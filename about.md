## About StoxFeed

StoxFeed is an independent publication tracking the pulse of the stock market and financial markets.

We cover daily developments in equities, earnings reports, market trends, sector rotations, and the forces moving stock prices. From earnings surprises to Fed decisions and IPO filings, our goal is simple:

> Capture the moves, signals, and ideas shaping the markets.

Financial markets evolve rapidly. New catalysts emerge. Sectors rotate. Macro conditions shift. StoxFeed exists to observe, document, and analyze those changes with clarity and perspective.

## Why This Exists

StoxFeed is an experimental publishing project.

It was created to explore a focused, signal-over-noise approach to market reporting — short, relevant, and structured coverage without hype or clutter.

We are testing formats, tone, and frequency while building a resource that professionals in finance, trading, and market analysis can rely on.


## Contribute

This project is open and evolving.

If you:

- Work in finance, trading, or market analysis
- Have insights on market trends or sector dynamics
- Want to share analysis, commentary, or curated news
- Or simply believe market coverage deserves sharper reporting

We welcome contributions.

StoxFeed is about tracking what drives the markets — and the people shaping them.
