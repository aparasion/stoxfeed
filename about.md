## About StoxFeed

StoxFeed is an independent publication tracking the pulse of global financial markets.

We cover daily developments in equities, earnings reports, crypto, commodities, sector rotations, IPOs, M&A activity, and the macro forces that move prices. From earnings surprises and Fed rate decisions to Bitcoin milestones and oil shocks, our goal is simple:

> Capture the moves, signals, and ideas shaping the markets.

Financial markets evolve rapidly. New catalysts emerge. Sectors rotate. Central banks act. Crypto narratives shift. StoxFeed exists to observe, document, and analyze those changes with clarity and perspective — across every asset class that matters.

## What We Cover

- **Equities** — Stock moves, sector trends, earnings results, and analyst calls across US and global markets
- **Crypto** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, altcoins, DeFi, and the institutional adoption reshaping digital assets
- **Macro & Policy** — Fed decisions, ECB statements, interest rate outlooks, and the economic data driving them
- **IPOs & Deals** — New listings, M&A activity, private equity moves, and capital market developments
- **Commodities & FX** — Oil, energy, and currency market developments tied to broader macro themes

## Why This Exists

StoxFeed is a focused, signal-over-noise approach to market reporting — short, relevant, and structured coverage without hype or clutter.

We publish daily to give finance professionals, traders, and market-watchers a fast read on what moved, why it moved, and what to watch next.
