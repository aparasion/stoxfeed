## About HealthspanWire

HealthspanWire is an independent publication tracking the frontiers of longevity science and healthspan research.

We cover daily developments in aging biology, longevity therapeutics, biomarkers, nutrition science, and the technologies extending human healthspan. From senolytic breakthroughs to clinical trial results and regulatory shifts, our goal is simple:

> Capture the discoveries, movements, and ideas shaping the future of human longevity.

The longevity field evolves rapidly. New therapies emerge. Biomarkers redefine how we measure aging. Technology transforms what's possible. HealthspanWire exists to observe, document, and analyze those changes with clarity and perspective.

## Why This Exists

HealthspanWire is an experimental publishing project.

It was created to explore a focused, signal-over-noise approach to longevity reporting — short, relevant, and structured coverage without hype or clutter.

We are testing formats, tone, and frequency while building a resource that professionals in longevity science, biotech, and healthspan research can rely on.
