In a recent webinar, leaders from Kayak and Pega discussed the complexities of language operations as organizations scale, emphasizing the need for a strategic approach to migrating language technology stacks. They highlighted how outdated systems can lead to inefficiencies, requiring teams to rethink their workflows and align them with broader business goals rather than simply transferring existing processes.

This discussion is crucial for localization and language services professionals, as it underscores the importance of viewing migration not just as a technical upgrade but as an opportunity for operational transformation. By carefully mapping workflows and addressing change management, organizations can enhance their scalability and efficiency, ultimately leading to measurable improvements in project turnaround and collaboration across departments.

For teams considering migration, the key takeaway is to prioritize alignment with business objectives and invest in thorough workflow mapping. This approach fosters a smoother transition and positions organizations for long-term success. I highly recommend checking out the full webinar for deeper insights and practical strategies.
