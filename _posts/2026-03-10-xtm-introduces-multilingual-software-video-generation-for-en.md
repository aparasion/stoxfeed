XTM International has launched the Software Recorder, a groundbreaking tool that enables enterprises to generate multilingual software videos at scale. By integrating XTM's Rigi and Video Creation Cloud, this new capability allows teams to capture UI interactions once and automatically replicate them across localized versions, streamlining the video production process significantly.

This development is crucial for the localization and language services industry as it addresses the common challenge of keeping instructional videos up-to-date with rapidly changing software UIs. With Software Recorder, organizations can save at least 60% of their time on video production, transforming it from a costly endeavor into a scalable content channel. The seamless integration with translation management systems ensures consistency in terminology and tone across all languages, enhancing the overall quality of localized content.

For localization professionals, the Software Recorder represents a significant leap forward in efficiency and effectiveness. I highly recommend exploring the full story to understand how this innovation can impact your workflows and drive global growth.
