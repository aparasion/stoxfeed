XTM International and Vistatec have announced a strategic partnership to launch an enterprise AI globalisation service aimed at enhancing the production of global content for organizations. This collaboration combines XTM's AI-driven globalisation platform with Vistatec's expert translation and localisation services, providing a comprehensive solution that ensures governance, auditability, and operational efficiency across teams and markets.

The partnership addresses the challenges enterprises face in scaling global content quickly while maintaining brand integrity and compliance. By integrating AI with human expertise, the service covers the entire content lifecycle, from intake and governance to quality control and reporting. This streamlined approach aims to reduce duplication of efforts and improve visibility into global content performance, ultimately positioning globalisation as a key driver of growth.
