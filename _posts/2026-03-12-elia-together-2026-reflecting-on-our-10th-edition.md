The ELIA Community convened on February 19-20, 2026, at the Crowne Plaza Porto for ELIA Together 2026, an event focused on collaboration and co-creation within the language industry. Attendees, including freelancers, LSPs, and academics, engaged in discussions around building stronger partnerships and navigating the evolving landscape of localization. The atmosphere was described as open and welcoming, fostering meaningful connections among participants.

The event featured a diverse program that included a keynote on adaptability, practical workshops, and panel discussions addressing collaboration frameworks, networking strategies, and the integration of AI in localization workflows. The emphasis on human connection and community was a standout theme, with many participants noting the importance of intentional collaboration in driving growth and innovation in the industry.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the renewed focus on building genuine relationships and co-creating solutions. I highly recommend exploring the full insights from ELIA Together 2026 to understand how these discussions can influence your work and partnerships.
