XTM International has announced its acquisition of Consoltec, a Montreal-based company known for its FlowFit project management system tailored for large enterprises. This strategic move enhances XTM's leadership in the translation business management system (TBMS) sector by integrating FlowFit with its existing XTRF platform, which aids language service providers in improving profitability and client services.

The combined offerings will allow businesses to standardize best practices and streamline complex processes, providing greater visibility and control over translation and localization operations. Ian Evans, CEO of XTM, emphasized that the integration will deliver a unique solution that meets the growing demand for secure, high-quality multilingual content.

Consoltec's expertise in developing project management systems for regulated industries, alongside XTM's innovative AI technology, aims to create a centralized platform that meets the needs of global organizations. This acquisition follows a significant year for XTM, which recently launched an Advanced AI Pack and was recognized as the AI Localization Software Company of the Year by CIOReview.
