The Federal Reserve Board has approved Home BancShares' application to acquire Mountain Commerce Bancorp, which includes Mountain Commerce Bank in Knoxville, Tennessee. This strategic merger, announced on March 12, 2026, also permits Centennial Bank, a subsidiary of Home BancShares, to merge with Mountain Commerce Bank and establish a branch at its location.

This development is significant for the banking sector, as it underscores ongoing consolidation trends in regional banking. The merger is expected to enhance Home BancShares' market presence in Tennessee and could lead to increased efficiencies and improved service offerings. Investors will be keen to monitor how this acquisition impacts earnings and market share in a competitive landscape.
