Crowdin has conducted an experiment to uncover the true costs of AI localization, revealing significant insights for the language services industry. Unlike many platforms that impose surcharges, Crowdin offers direct API rates from providers like OpenAI and Google, making it a cost-effective choice for businesses looking to leverage AI for translation.

The findings indicate that the cost of translating different content types varies, with mobile app UI translations being 23% to 73% more expensive than standard articles due to the complexity of processing metadata. Additionally, incorporating visual context in marketing emails, while increasing costs, significantly enhances translation accuracy and reduces errors. This highlights the importance of understanding content complexity and context in budgeting for localization projects.

For localization professionals, the key takeaway is the value of using a comprehensive platform like Crowdin, which not only minimizes costs but also integrates AI capabilities with human proofreading and seamless content syncing.
