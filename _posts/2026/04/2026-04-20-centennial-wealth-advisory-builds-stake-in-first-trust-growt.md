Centennial Wealth Advisory LLC significantly bolstered its investment in the First Trust Growth Strength ETF (FTGS), increasing its position by 91,927 shares in Q1 2026, valued at $2.97 million. This acquisition now represents 1.79% of Centennial's total assets under management, highlighting a strategic move towards growth-oriented investments amid a competitive market landscape.

The FTGS ETF, which focuses on U.S. equities and REITs with strong growth profiles, has delivered a one-year return of 29.2%, underperforming the S&P 500 by 5.5 percentage points. Its disciplined, rules-based investment strategy aims to capture growth opportunities while maintaining financial strength and liquidity, making it appealing for investors seeking stability in volatile markets. The ETF's selective approach could provide an edge in environments where earnings stability is prioritized over speculative growth.

For market professionals, Centennial's increased stake in FTGS signals confidence in a targeted growth strategy, particularly as interest rates and market dynamics evolve. This move may prompt a closer examination of growth-focused ETFs that emphasize financial strength in their selection criteria.
