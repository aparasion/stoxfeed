RaveDAO's RAVE token has plummeted nearly 90% in just 24 hours, collapsing from a peak of $27.33 to $1.15 and erasing over $5 billion in market capitalization. This dramatic decline follows investigations by crypto exchanges Binance and Bitget into suspicious trading activities that preceded the token's meteoric rise. Allegations suggest that approximately 90% of RAVE's total supply is concentrated in three wallets linked to the team, with significant transfers to exchanges occurring just before the price surge.

The fallout from this incident highlights the risks associated with concentrated token holdings and the potential for market manipulation. The rapid price increase, which saw RAVE surge by 10,800% in days, triggered $44 million in liquidations, primarily affecting short sellers. RaveDAO has denied any wrongdoing but has not addressed specific allegations regarding the token distribution or trading patterns.

Investors should be cautious as the ongoing investigations could lead to further volatility in RAVE and similar tokens. The situation underscores the importance of transparency and sound governance in the crypto space, particularly for projects with significant market movements.
