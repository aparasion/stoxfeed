In a strategic pivot for AI investment, a seasoned portfolio manager highlights five key stocks that stand out for new investors entering the AI space. Among these, Nvidia (NVDA) and Broadcom (AVGO) emerge as top choices due to their current profitability from semiconductor sales, positioning them as essential players in the AI infrastructure build-out. As companies invest heavily in AI, these firms are set to benefit from sustained demand for their chips, ensuring long-term growth.

Additionally, tech giants Microsoft (MSFT) and Alphabet (GOOGL) are recommended for their robust cloud computing platforms, Azure and Google Cloud, which are critical for AI deployment. Their usage-based business models promise increasing cash flows as AI adoption accelerates. Meanwhile, Nebius (NBIS) is highlighted as a high-risk, high-reward option, with significant growth projections tied to its GPU-based AI computing focus.

Investors should consider these stocks as foundational elements for an AI-focused portfolio, particularly given the ongoing demand for AI infrastructure and services.
