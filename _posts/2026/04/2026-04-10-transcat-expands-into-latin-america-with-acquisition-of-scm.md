Transcat, Inc. has successfully acquired SCM Metrology and Laboratories S.A. for approximately $13 million, marking its entry into the Latin American market. This transaction, which closed on April 9, 2026, enhances Transcat's operational footprint and aligns with its strategy to support customers in high-growth, regulated sectors, particularly in medical devices and life sciences.

The acquisition is significant for Transcat as it not only broadens its service capabilities but also leverages SCM's established relationships with multinational companies, many of which are already part of Transcat's customer base. This strategic move is expected to drive additional distribution sales and strengthen the company's competitive positioning in a region that has seen substantial foreign investment due to favorable government incentives.

Investors should note that this expansion could lead to increased revenue streams for Transcat, particularly as management discusses the implications during the upcoming Fourth Quarter Fiscal Year 2026 Earnings Conference Call.
