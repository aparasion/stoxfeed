Veris Residential, Inc. has raised its full-year core FFO guidance for the second consecutive quarter, now projecting $0.67–$0.68 per share, a 12.5% increase over 2024. This revision is driven by successful tax appeal outcomes and robust operational performance, including a 3.9% blended net rental growth for the quarter. The company also reported a significant reduction in net debt-to-EBITDA, down 15% year-to-date to 10x, with expectations to decrease further to around 9x following the anticipated closing of the Harborside 8/9 sale.

The financial markets should note Veris's strategic focus on asset sales, having surpassed its initial target by $150 million, now aiming for $650 million in non-strategic asset dispositions. This proactive approach not only strengthens the balance sheet but also positions the company favorably against peers in a challenging multifamily market, where demand remains resilient in key areas like Jersey City.

Investors should consider Veris's enhanced liquidity and operational efficiency as indicators of its capacity to navigate market pressures while capitalizing on favorable regional dynamics, particularly in the Northeast.
