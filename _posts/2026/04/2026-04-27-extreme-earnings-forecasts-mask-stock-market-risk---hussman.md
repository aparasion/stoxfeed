On March 31, 2026, the Hussman Strategic Total Return Fund and Hussman Strategic Allocation Fund announced income distributions of $0.0675 and $0.0324 per share, respectively. This payout reflects the funds' ongoing commitment to providing returns to investors amid changing market conditions.

Additionally, effective November 1, 2024, the Hussman Strategic Allocation Fund will rebrand as the Strategic Market Cycle Fund, aligning with the SEC's new "names rule" for mutual funds. This rule aims to clarify fund objectives by restricting names that imply a specific investment strategy, such as "growth" or "value." The new name emphasizes the fund's value-conscious approach and distinguishes it from passive, index-tracking options.

For market professionals, the rebranding and income distributions signal a strategic shift that may attract investors seeking a disciplined, full-cycle investment strategy, potentially impacting fund flows and overall market positioning.
