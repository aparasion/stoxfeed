OraSure Technologies Inc. (OSUR) experienced a surge in options trading today, with 3,510 contracts exchanged, equating to about 351,000 underlying shares—62.6% of its average daily volume. Notably, the $2.50 strike call option expiring on April 17, 2026, accounted for 3,500 of these contracts, indicating heightened investor interest in potential upward movement for the stock.

The $14 strike put option expiring on May 15, 2026, was particularly active, with 5,002 contracts traded, suggesting a significant focus on downside protection or bearish sentiment regarding VRDN's future performance.

The increased options activity in both stocks highlights a potential shift in market sentiment, with traders positioning themselves for specific price movements.
