Three companies are set to report earnings after hours on April 1, 2026, with varying expectations that could impact their stock performance. Penguin Solutions, Inc. (PENG) anticipates earnings per share (EPS) of $0.28, reflecting a 20% decline year-over-year. Despite this forecast, PENG has consistently exceeded expectations over the past year, notably beating consensus by 44% in the last quarter. 

In contrast, Franklin Covey Company (FC) is projected to report a break-even EPS, a significant turnaround from the previous year’s loss, representing a 100% increase. However, FC has struggled recently, missing EPS estimates by nearly 21% last quarter. Bassett Furniture Industries, Incorporated (BSET) expects an EPS of $0.17, down 19% year-over-year, and has also missed prior estimates.

Market professionals should closely monitor these earnings reports, particularly PENG's potential to maintain its positive momentum and FC's ability to justify its higher-than-average Price to Earnings ratio within its industry.
