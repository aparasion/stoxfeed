Bitcoin (BTC) remains stagnant, trading flat while altcoins like NEAR Protocol (NEAR) and Avalanche (AVAX) see notable gains of 5.8% and 3.6%, respectively. The CoinDesk 20 Index, which tracks a broad spectrum of cryptocurrencies, is currently at 1909.43, reflecting a modest increase of 0.7% since Thursday. With nineteen out of twenty assets in the index rising, the altcoin rally highlights a shift in market momentum away from Bitcoin.

This divergence in performance is significant for market professionals as it may indicate a growing interest in altcoins, potentially reshaping portfolio strategies. The robust gains in NEAR and AVAX could attract institutional investors looking for diversification beyond Bitcoin, which has struggled to gain traction. 

As the cryptocurrency landscape evolves, the performance of altcoins could signal a new trend in asset allocation, prompting traders to reassess their positions and strategies in light of shifting investor sentiment.
