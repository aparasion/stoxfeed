OpenAI's potential IPO is generating buzz, but for investors looking to gain exposure, Microsoft (MSFT) presents a compelling opportunity. With a 27% stake in OpenAI, valued at approximately $230 billion, Microsoft is positioned as a key player in the AI sector. This stake represents nearly 10% of Microsoft's total market cap of $2.8 trillion, underscoring the significance of OpenAI's future prospects for Microsoft’s overall valuation.

As OpenAI approaches what could be its final private funding round, the urgency for a public offering increases, especially given its recent $122 billion capital raise that valued the company at around $852 billion. Investors should note that Microsoft’s current operating price-to-earnings ratio is at a decade low, meaning the core business is undervalued while the OpenAI stake could be seen as an added bonus.

For market professionals, now may be an opportune time to consider Microsoft shares, as they offer a unique entry point into the burgeoning AI market without the risks associated with investing in a private entity.
