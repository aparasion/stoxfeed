The economic calendar today highlights key data releases and corporate earnings that could influence market sentiment. Notably, the U.S. will release the ADP employment change at 1:15 PM GMT, with expectations of a modest increase from the previous 54.75k. Later, the house price index and consumer confidence figures will be scrutinized, with the latter expected to decline to 89 from 91.8. Additionally, the Richmond Fed index is anticipated to show a slight improvement.

Earnings reports from major companies like General Motors, Coca-Cola, and UPS are set to be released before the U.S. market opens, while Visa and Starbucks will report after the close. These results could impact their respective sectors and provide insights into consumer spending trends, especially in light of potential shifts in economic indicators.

Market participants should pay close attention to these data points and earnings releases, as they may signal shifts in consumer confidence and economic health, influencing trading strategies in the coming sessions.
