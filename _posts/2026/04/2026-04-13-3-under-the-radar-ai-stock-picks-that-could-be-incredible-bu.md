Investors are increasingly eyeing smaller companies with significant growth potential, despite the inherent risks. Three stocks stand out for their potential upside: IonQ (IONQ), SoundHound AI (SOUN), and Nebius (NBIS). Each of these firms operates in high-growth sectors—quantum computing and AI—where the promise of innovation is tempered by competitive pressures and execution risks.

IonQ leads in quantum computing with the most accurate technology available, reporting a staggering 429% revenue increase in Q4 2025. SoundHound AI is carving out a niche in automating customer interactions through generative AI, achieving 59% revenue growth last quarter. Meanwhile, Nebius is positioned to capitalize on the AI boom, projecting its annual run rate to soar from $1.25 billion to up to $9 billion by 2026, thanks in part to its partnership with Nvidia.

For market professionals, these stocks represent a blend of high risk and potential high reward. Investors should weigh the volatility against the transformative potential of these technologies as they consider their next moves.
