British American Tobacco PLC (BAT) has appointed Dragos Constantinescu as its new Chief Financial Officer and Executive Director, effective September 1. Constantinescu, who previously served as CEO of Asahi Europe & International, brings extensive experience from his 16 years at BAT in various senior finance and management roles. He will replace Javed Iqbal, who will transition to Director of Digital & Information after serving as Interim CFO.

This leadership change comes at a pivotal moment for BAT as it undergoes a transformation aimed at sustainable growth and improving cash returns. Constantinescu's background in both the tobacco and beverage sectors positions him well to navigate the complexities of BAT's shift towards reduced-risk products and innovation in its portfolio.

For market professionals, Constantinescu's appointment signals a continued commitment to strategic transformation at BAT, which may influence investor sentiment and stock performance as the company aims to enhance its growth trajectory in a challenging regulatory environment.
