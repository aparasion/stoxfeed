Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang's assertion that AI agents will revolutionize various sectors resonates with Coinbase Global, which sees potential in the intersection of AI and cryptocurrency. Highlighting three cryptocurrencies poised to benefit from this trend, Bittensor emerges as a frontrunner. This decentralized blockchain protocol, designed for sharing and training large language models, has gained over 45% this year, boasting a market cap of $3.5 billion and hosting significant AI projects like MyShell.

Kite, labeled as the first AI payment blockchain, has surged 69% this year and attracted Coinbase's investment, enhancing its visibility and accessibility to investors. Meanwhile, Virtuals Protocol, once a top performer, has faced volatility, trading at an 87% discount from its all-time high. 

For market professionals, the key takeaway is that while investing in AI-focused cryptocurrencies presents high-risk opportunities, established players like Coinbase may offer a more stable investment route as they integrate AI capabilities into their platforms.
