Leopold Aschenbrenner's venture fund, Situational Awareness, has surged to prominence with a focus on artificial general intelligence (AGI), amassing $5.5 billion in assets since its launch in 2024. Aschenbrenner, a former OpenAI employee, emphasizes the transformative potential of AI, asserting that many investors are unaware of the impending advancements. The fund's portfolio consists of 24 stocks primarily centered on AI infrastructure, which have collectively outperformed the S&P 500 this year.

Key holdings include Bloom Energy, which has seen a staggering 150% increase in stock price, and Lumentum Holdings, up 143%. Other notable investments include CoreWeave and Core Scientific, both transitioning from cryptocurrency to AI support, reflecting a broader trend in the tech sector. This shift highlights the growing demand for AI-related infrastructure and services, positioning these companies as critical players in the evolving market landscape.

For market professionals, the rapid growth of these lesser-known AI support stocks signals an opportunity to diversify portfolios beyond conventional tech giants, as the AI sector continues to expand and innovate.
