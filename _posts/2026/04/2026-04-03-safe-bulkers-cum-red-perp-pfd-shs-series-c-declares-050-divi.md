Safe Bulkers, Inc. has announced a quarterly dividend of $0.50 per share for its Cum Red Perp Pfd Shs Series C (SB.PR.C), maintaining the same rate as previous distributions. This dividend, which offers a forward yield of 7.69%, is payable on April 30 to shareholders on record as of April 17, with the ex-dividend date also set for April 17.

This consistent dividend payout reflects Safe Bulkers' stable cash flow amid ongoing operational expansion plans, including a target to grow its fleet to 38 Phase 3 vessels by Q1 2029 while maintaining a robust liquidity position of $382 million. Such financial stability is crucial for investors looking for reliable income streams, especially in the current volatile market environment.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is that Safe Bulkers continues to demonstrate solid dividend reliability, which may appeal to income-focused investors and could influence stock performance as the company navigates its growth trajectory.
