Chewy Inc. (CHWY) is emerging as a compelling growth stock, currently trading at around $27, despite its modest share price. The company, a leading online retailer for pet products, is capitalizing on significant growth opportunities through its Autoship subscription service, retail media advertising, and expanding healthcare business. Notably, 84% of Chewy's sales in Q4 were from Autoship, showcasing strong customer retention and cost management.

The growth in Chewy's advertising segment, fueled by first-party data, is enhancing operating margins, while the recent acquisition of 29 Vet Care clinics positions the company to tap into high-margin healthcare services. This strategic integration not only attracts customers but also enhances the overall profitability of Chewy's ecosystem. 

Investors should note that Chewy's adjusted EBITDA margin is on an upward trajectory, with management targeting a long-term goal of 10%. Given its current valuation at 11.4 times the midpoint of EBITDA guidance, Chewy presents an attractive investment opportunity for those seeking growth potential in the market.
