Vertiv Holdings (VRT) has emerged as a compelling investment opportunity within the artificial intelligence (AI) sector, particularly for those looking to capitalize on the infrastructure supporting AI data centers. The company specializes in power delivery and cooling systems, essential components for data center operations, and has reported impressive growth metrics. In the fourth quarter, Vertiv's organic revenue surged 19% year-over-year, while organic orders skyrocketed 252%, indicating a robust demand driven by increased spending from AI hyperscalers.

This strong performance is reflected in Vertiv's expanding backlog, which grew 109%, positioning the company well for sustained growth through 2030 as the AI build-out continues. Analysts project a 34% growth rate for 2026, supported by recurring subscription revenue from its software offerings. However, with a forward P/E ratio of 43, the stock carries a premium valuation that may temper its attractiveness compared to other options in the sector.

For market professionals, Vertiv represents a unique play on AI infrastructure, with the potential for market-beating returns, albeit with caution warranted due to its current valuation.
