Strategy (MSTR) has emerged as a significant player in the Bitcoin space, purchasing $250 million worth of Bitcoin in August 2020 and now holding over 815,000 units, making it the largest corporate holder of the cryptocurrency. Despite being 64% off its peak, MSTR shares have skyrocketed 1,170% since that initial investment, significantly outperforming both Bitcoin and the S&P 500. The company operates almost like a leveraged Bitcoin ETF, raising capital through various means to expand its Bitcoin holdings.

This unique approach has attracted long-term Bitcoin bulls willing to embrace higher risk for potentially greater returns. Strategy's business model focuses on increasing its Bitcoin per share (BPS) rather than traditional earnings metrics, with BPS rising 74% in 2024 alone. However, the volatility associated with this strategy is pronounced, as evidenced by MSTR's current price being significantly lower than its all-time high.

For market professionals, Strategy represents both an innovative investment vehicle and a high-risk proposition. Understanding its capital-raising strategies and risk management will be crucial for those considering exposure to this amplified Bitcoin play.
