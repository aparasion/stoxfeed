Bitcoin is currently facing significant resistance as buyers attempt to push its price toward the $76,000 level, while bears are actively defending against upward momentum. Despite a mixed week for U.S. spot BTC exchange-traded funds, with net inflows of $576.5 million, analysts suggest that Bitcoin must surpass key levels at $78,000 and $81,600 to establish a sustainable recovery. Until then, the outlook remains cautious, with potential selling pressure looming.

The performance of Bitcoin is critical for the broader cryptocurrency market, as its movements often dictate the direction of major altcoins. For instance, Ether is showing signs of potential recovery, with support at $2,200 and a possible rally above $2,400 if it breaks key resistance levels. Conversely, altcoins like XRP and BNB are struggling against bearish sentiment, indicating a challenging environment for traders.

Market professionals should closely monitor Bitcoin's ability to break through the $76,000 resistance, as a successful move could trigger a broader rally across altcoins, while failure to do so may lead to increased selling pressure and further declines.
