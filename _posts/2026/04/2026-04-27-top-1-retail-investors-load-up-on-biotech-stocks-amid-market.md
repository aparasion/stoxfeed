Hashed is set to host "Nitro Seoul Game Day" on April 30, marking the inaugural demo day for its AI-native builder program. This event will showcase four startups that have developed innovative products over an intensive eight-week period, highlighting the growing intersection of AI and entrepreneurship in South Korea.

The significance of this event extends to the broader financial markets, as it underscores the increasing investment in AI technologies, which has been a driving force behind recent rallies in tech stocks. The KOSPI index recently surpassed the 6,600 mark, buoyed by strong performances from major players like Samsung Electronics and SK Hynix, both of which are heavily invested in AI and semiconductor advancements. This enthusiasm reflects a robust investor sentiment towards the tech sector, particularly in light of ongoing global AI trends.

For market professionals, the "Nitro Seoul Game Day" serves as a reminder of the potential for innovation-driven growth in the tech sector, suggesting that continued investment in AI startups could yield significant returns as they mature and scale their operations.
