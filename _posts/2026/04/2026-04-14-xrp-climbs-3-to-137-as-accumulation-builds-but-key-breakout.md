XRP has gained 3% to reach $1.37, driven by strong trading volume and rising demand, despite prevailing bearish sentiment. The token is currently consolidating just below recent highs after breaking above the $1.35 resistance level, with traders eyeing $1.42 to $1.45 as the critical breakout zone. Historically, a similar combination of rising prices and strong volume amidst negative sentiment has preceded significant rallies.

The current technical setup suggests steady accumulation rather than speculative trading, with XRP maintaining higher lows. However, the market remains cautious, as a drop below $1.33 could shift the focus back to downside risks. The overall structure indicates that XRP is nearing a pivotal breakout decision point, with pressure building for a potential larger move.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is to monitor the $1.35 support level closely; maintaining this could lead to a breakout above $1.42, while failure to hold could signal a reversal in momentum.
