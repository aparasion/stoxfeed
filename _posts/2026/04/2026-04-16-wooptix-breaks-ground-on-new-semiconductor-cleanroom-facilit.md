Wooptix has announced the groundbreaking of a new cleanroom facility in Tenerife, Spain, aimed at enhancing its research, development, and manufacturing capabilities in semiconductor metrology technologies. This facility will serve as a central hub for assembling new equipment and conducting testing and validation, reinforcing Spain’s semiconductor ecosystem and contributing to Europe’s technological independence in this critical sector.

The strategic investment not only positions Wooptix for sustained growth in the global semiconductor market but also offers logistical and operational advantages. The facility's coastal location will facilitate the efficient transport of sensitive machinery, while its alignment with EU regulations provides access to incentives that promote industrial innovation. With construction set to begin shortly, the facility is expected to be fully operational by year-end, further supporting Wooptix’s Phemet® metrology system, which addresses the increasing demand for precision in high-volume manufacturing.

For market professionals, this development signals a strengthening of the European semiconductor landscape, potentially impacting supply chains and competitive dynamics within the industry.
