Nova Minerals Limited (NVA) has appointed Ashlie Thorburn as its new Chief Financial Officer, effective April 20, 2026. With over 20 years of financial leadership experience in the mining sector, Thorburn's background includes significant roles at Southwest Critical Materials and Armanino Advisory, where she focused on finance transformation and public company readiness. Her expertise in managing financial operations during critical transitions positions her well to guide Nova through its next growth phase.

This leadership change comes at a pivotal time for Nova Minerals as it seeks to enhance its financial reporting and internal controls, crucial for attracting investors and supporting its strategic initiatives. Thorburn's track record in both public and private sectors suggests a strong potential for improved operational efficiency and financial stability, which could positively influence the company's stock performance.

Market professionals should monitor how Thorburn's appointment impacts Nova's financial strategies and operational execution, especially as the mining sector continues to navigate fluctuating commodity prices and investor expectations.
