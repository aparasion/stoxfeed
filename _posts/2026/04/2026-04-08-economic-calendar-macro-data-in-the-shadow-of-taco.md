Today's market activity is largely influenced by geopolitical tensions, particularly in the Middle East, overshadowing the light economic calendar that features only the EIA oil inventory change and the minutes from the March FOMC meeting. Traders are closely monitoring the potential for a quick resolution in ongoing conflicts, which has led to pronounced movements in TACO-related assets.

Market professionals should be prepared for volatility driven by geopolitical events, particularly if negotiations in the Middle East shift significantly. This could lead to fluctuations in oil prices and related equities, making it crucial to stay attuned to both market sentiment and geopolitical news.
