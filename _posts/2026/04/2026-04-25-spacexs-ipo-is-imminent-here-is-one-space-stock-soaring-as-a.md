SpaceX's anticipated IPO has sparked a rally in space stocks, with Intuitive Machines (NASDAQ: LUNR) seeing a notable 32.4% increase over the past month. As a key player in the space infrastructure sector, Intuitive Machines has secured significant contracts with NASA, including a $180.4 million deal to support the Artemis program, which aims to establish a permanent lunar base. However, the company’s financials remain precarious, with a net margin of -51.58% for 2025 and a reliance on government contracts for revenue.

Despite these challenges, Intuitive Machines holds a stronger cash position than many of its peers, boasting $582.6 million in cash against $553.4 million in total liabilities. This financial cushion, coupled with its operational expertise in lunar missions, positions it as a speculative investment option for those looking to capitalize on the growing interest in the space sector.

Investors should approach Intuitive Machines with caution, considering its volatility and financial instability, while keeping an eye on its potential upside as the space industry evolves.
