Nuclear energy is gaining momentum as countries prioritize energy independence and cleaner power sources, with NuScale Power and BWX Technologies at the forefront. NuScale is developing small modular reactor (SMR) technology aimed at simplifying and reducing the costs of nuclear deployment, while BWX Technologies serves as the sole nuclear fuel provider for the U.S. Navy, solidifying its role as a critical national security asset.

The demand for electricity in the U.S. is projected to grow at 2.5% annually over the next decade, significantly impacting the nuclear sector. While NuScale's innovative Power Modules promise future potential, their commercial deployment is still years away, with the first plants not expected until 2033. In contrast, BWX Technologies has demonstrated robust performance, securing $2.6 billion in new defense contracts and a 50% increase in its backlog, indicating strong revenue visibility.

For investors, BWX Technologies currently presents a more compelling opportunity given its established government contracts and immediate revenue generation, compared to NuScale's long-term growth trajectory.
