SmartFinancial, Inc. reported a strong start to 2026, with tangible book value rising to $27.33 per share, up from $26.86 at year-end. The company achieved operating earnings of $13.7 million, or $0.81 per diluted share, alongside operating revenue of $53.8 million, reflecting a robust 14% annualized growth in loans and a 7% increase in core deposits. Management highlighted a disciplined approach to expenses, keeping operating noninterest expenses at $32.9 million, which is below prior guidance.

These results underscore SmartFinancial's commitment to organic growth, as evidenced by a stable loan portfolio and a low nonperforming asset ratio of just 0.25%. The company is also adapting to competitive pressures in the lending market while maintaining a strong net interest margin of 3.48%, aided by lower funding costs. 

A key takeaway for market professionals is SmartFinancial's focus on organic growth strategies and efficiency improvements, which may position the firm favorably against competitors amid rising market challenges.
