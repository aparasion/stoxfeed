Infrastrutture Wireless Italiane S.p.A. (INW.MI) reported a solid increase in full-year 2025 net income, reaching 360.8 million euros, up from 353.8 million euros the previous year. The company also saw gains in EBIT and EBITDA, with figures of 578.3 million euros and 984.4 million euros, respectively. A proposed dividend of 0.5543 euros per share, reflecting a 7.5% increase, aligns with its commitment to shareholder returns.

This performance signals positive momentum in the telecommunications infrastructure sector, particularly as revenue climbed to 1.08 billion euros. The company's forecast for 2026 indicates stable revenue expectations between 1.05 billion and 1.09 billion euros, with an EBITDA margin around 90%. The consistent dividend policy and projected financial leverage of about 5.5x suggest a robust operational framework.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is the reaffirmation of Infrastrutture Wireless Italiane's commitment to shareholder value through dividends, alongside steady revenue growth, which could enhance investor confidence in the stock.
