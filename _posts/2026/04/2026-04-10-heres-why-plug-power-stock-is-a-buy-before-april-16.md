Plug Power (NASDAQ: PLUG) has rebounded dramatically over the past year, with its stock surging over 130% as the company stabilizes its revenue and narrows losses amid a recovering hydrogen market. Analysts project an 18% compound annual growth rate (CAGR) for revenue from $710 million in 2025 to $1.15 billion by 2028, bolstered by cost-cutting initiatives like "Project Quantum Leap." With a market cap of $3.8 billion, Plug Power remains attractively valued at under five times this year's sales.

The upcoming Reddit AMA session on April 16, hosted by new CEO Luis Crespo, marks a strategic shift in engaging retail investors and could further boost interest in the stock. Crespo's focus on the company's strategic direction and operational execution signals a commitment to transparency and growth, which may resonate with both retail and institutional investors.

For market professionals, the combination of insider buying, high short interest, and Crespo's outreach strategy suggests that Plug Power could be positioned for a potential short squeeze, making it an intriguing stock to consider accumulating now.
