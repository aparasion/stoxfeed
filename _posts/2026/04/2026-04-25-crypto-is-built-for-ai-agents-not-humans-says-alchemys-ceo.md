Alchemy CEO Nikil Viswanathan asserts that the future of commerce will be dominated by AI agents operating within a crypto framework, fundamentally reshaping the financial landscape. He argues that traditional finance was designed with human limitations in mind—constraints like geography and operating hours—which are increasingly becoming bottlenecks as AI agents begin to transact autonomously and continuously across borders.

The implications for the financial markets are significant. As AI agents leverage the seamless, borderless nature of crypto, they will require infrastructure that allows for instantaneous, programmable transactions without the friction of traditional finance. This shift could lead to a more efficient financial system, where the complexity that hinders human users becomes an asset for AI, enabling real-time capital management and optimized transaction flows.

Market professionals should consider the potential for crypto to evolve from an alternative financial system to the foundational infrastructure for AI-driven economic activity. This transition could redefine transaction models and investment strategies, highlighting the need for adaptability in a rapidly changing financial environment.
