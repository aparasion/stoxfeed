Straight Path Wealth Management has increased its stake in the Dimensional Global Core Plus Fixed Income ETF (DFGP), adding 53,257 shares valued at approximately $2.8 million, according to an SEC filing dated April 22, 2026. This adjustment brings their total holding to 488,899 shares, representing 7.1% of their total assets under management (AUM) of $374.4 million as of March 31.

This move underscores the growing confidence in fixed-income securities, particularly as the ETF provides a globally diversified portfolio aimed at enhanced risk-adjusted returns. With a conservative investment strategy focused on investment-grade bonds, approximately 70% of the ETF's assets are rated A or higher, mitigating credit risk while maintaining exposure across various sectors and geographies. However, the longer maturity profile of the portfolio may expose it to greater interest rate risk, especially in a rising rate environment.

For market professionals, the increase in DFGP holdings signals a strategic pivot towards fixed income amid potential market volatility, highlighting the importance of bond allocation in a well-rounded investment strategy.
