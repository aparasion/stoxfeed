The FDA is set to announce its decision on two promising melanoma treatments, REPL (Replimune Group) and IOVA (Iovance Biotherapeutics), which could significantly impact investor sentiment in the biotech sector. Both companies are vying for approval of their respective therapies, with REPL's investigational drug focused on oncolytic immunotherapy and IOVA's product centered on tumor-infiltrating lymphocyte therapy. 

The outcomes of these approvals will not only influence the stock performance of REPL and IOVA but also reflect broader market confidence in biotech innovations. A positive decision could bolster momentum for similar biotech firms, while a setback might trigger a reevaluation of risk in the sector, especially among companies developing cancer therapies.

Investors should closely monitor the FDA announcement, as it could serve as a barometer for market sentiment towards biotech stocks, particularly in the oncology space, and may prompt strategic adjustments in portfolios focused on this high-stakes area.
