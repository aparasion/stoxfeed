NEAR Protocol (NEAR) surged 8.1% over the weekend, leading the CoinDesk 20 Index, which rose 3.5% to 1968.74. Avalanche (AVAX) also showed strong performance, climbing 5.5% since Friday. This uptick is significant as it reflects a broader positive trend in the cryptocurrency market, with 17 out of 20 assets in the index trading higher.

The performance of NEAR and AVAX could indicate growing investor confidence in certain altcoins, particularly as the overall crypto market shows resilience. The CoinDesk 20 Index's movement suggests a potential shift in market sentiment, which may influence trading strategies and portfolio allocations in the coming days. Notably, laggards like Bitcoin Cash (BCH) and Stellar (XLM) experienced minor declines, highlighting the volatility that can accompany sector shifts.

For market professionals, the takeaway is clear: the recent gains in NEAR and AVAX could signal emerging opportunities in altcoins, warranting closer monitoring of these assets as the market evolves.
