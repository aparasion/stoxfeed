Artificial intelligence (AI) stocks, once the darlings of the market, have recently faltered, contributing to a decline in the S&P 500. Investor enthusiasm has waned amid geopolitical tensions, such as the ongoing turmoil in Iran, and broader economic uncertainties, which typically dampen the appeal of growth stocks. This shift has led to increased market volatility, leaving many investors cautious about reinvesting in AI-focused companies.

Amid this backdrop, Amazon (AMZN) emerges as a resilient player. The e-commerce and cloud computing giant has consistently demonstrated strong earnings growth and a solid return on invested capital (ROIC). Notably, Amazon Web Services (AWS) not only leads in cloud solutions but also benefits from rising demand for both AI and non-AI products. As AWS continues to thrive, Amazon's diversified offerings position it well to weather economic fluctuations.

For market professionals, Amazon represents a strategic investment in uncertain times, combining robust fundamentals with a leading role in the AI sector, making it a stock worth holding for long-term growth.
