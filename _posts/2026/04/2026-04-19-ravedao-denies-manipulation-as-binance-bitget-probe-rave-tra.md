RaveDAO has publicly distanced itself from the recent volatility of its RAVE token, which experienced a meteoric rise from approximately $0.25 to nearly $28 before plummeting over 80%. The project’s statement comes amid allegations of market manipulation, with onchain investigator ZachXBT accusing RaveDAO of orchestrating a pump-and-dump scheme due to concentrated token holdings and suspicious trading patterns. Major exchanges, including Binance and Bitget, are now investigating the trading activity surrounding RAVE.

The implications for the broader crypto market are significant, as this incident highlights ongoing concerns about market integrity and the potential for manipulation in the decentralized finance space. With over 90% of RAVE’s supply reportedly controlled by insiders, the scrutiny from exchanges could lead to increased regulatory attention, affecting trading strategies and investor confidence across the sector.

As RaveDAO plans to sell unlocked tokens to fund operations, the market will be watching closely for how these developments impact RAVE's price trajectory and the overall sentiment in the crypto market.
