ASML Holding (NASDAQ: ASML) is reaffirming its critical role in the artificial intelligence (AI) landscape, with its extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography machines essential for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. As the AI chip market is projected to grow by 29% through 2030, ASML stands to benefit significantly, leveraging its unique position that generates approximately 25% of its revenue from maintenance services. Despite a slight revenue growth forecast for 2026, the stock has surged 85% over the past year, reflecting strong investor confidence.

Meanwhile, Vertiv (NYSE: VRT) is capitalizing on the booming data center market, with its cooling and power solutions experiencing a remarkable 28% revenue increase in 2025. As the liquid cooling sector is expected to grow at a 32% CAGR through 2033, Vertiv's stock has risen 225% over the past year, underscoring its pivotal role in supporting infrastructure for AI and data centers. 

Investors should consider the growth trajectories of both ASML and Vertiv as they navigate the AI supercycle, with ASML’s premium valuation and Vertiv’s expanding market share highlighting potential opportunities in this evolving landscape.
