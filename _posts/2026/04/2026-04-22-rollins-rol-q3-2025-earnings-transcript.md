Rollins, Inc. reported robust third-quarter results, showcasing a 12% year-over-year revenue growth and an impressive 7.2% organic growth across its service lines. The integration of Saela has exceeded expectations, contributing to double-digit revenue increases and improved margins. Adjusted EBITDA margin rose 120 basis points to 25.2%, driven by operational efficiencies and a favorable tax rate that decreased to 24.8%.

These results highlight Rollins' strong performance in both residential and commercial segments, with notable increases in revenues of 11.2% and 11.8%, respectively. The company’s disciplined capital allocation strategy is evident through a 30% rise in operating cash flow and a 31% increase in free cash flow, allowing for a 10% year-over-year dividend hike and a further 11% increase announced recently. 

For market professionals, Rollins’ continued focus on organic growth combined with strategic acquisitions positions it favorably for sustained momentum, making it a company to watch as it maintains its growth trajectory into the next fiscal period.
