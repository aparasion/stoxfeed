Inspirion Wealth Advisors has significantly increased its stake in the BlackRock ETF Trust II - iShares AAA CLO Active ETF (CLOA), acquiring an additional 63,417 shares valued at approximately $3.3 million. This move raises CLOA's representation in Inspirion's reportable assets to 3.64%, highlighting a strategic focus on high-quality fixed-income investments amid ongoing market volatility.

The decision to bolster CLOA aligns with a broader income strategy, as the ETF targets AAA-rated collateralized loan obligations, which provide a layer of protection and a compelling 5.2% dividend yield. This yield is notably higher than many traditional investment-grade bond ETFs, making CLOA an attractive option for income-seeking investors, especially in a rising interest rate environment. While CLOA has underperformed the S&P 500 over the past year, it serves as a yield-focused play rather than a growth investment.

For market professionals, Inspirion's increased investment underscores the potential of CLOs in a diversified portfolio, particularly for those looking to enhance income without compromising credit quality.
