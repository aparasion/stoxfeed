Poet Technologies (NASDAQ: POET) surged 9.2% on the last trading day of March, capping a 17.7% increase for the week following the release of strong fourth-quarter 2025 financial results. The company, which specializes in optical modules for data centers, reported a significant revenue jump to $341,202 from just $29,032 year-over-year, reflecting a strategic shift from development to execution. CEO Dr. Suresh Venkatesan highlighted this transition, supported by robust capital inflows and increasing commercial validation of their technology.

Despite the recent rally, Poet remains down over 3% since the beginning of the year, indicating potential room for growth as it continues to secure production orders, including a notable $5 million deal for optical engines. This performance positions Poet as a compelling option for investors focused on companies poised to support the burgeoning demands of AI-driven data centers.

For market professionals, Poet Technologies presents an intriguing growth opportunity, particularly in the context of increasing investment in AI infrastructure.
