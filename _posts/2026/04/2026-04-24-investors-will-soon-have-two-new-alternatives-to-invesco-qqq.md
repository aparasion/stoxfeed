The Invesco QQQ Trust, a leading ETF tracking the Nasdaq-100 index, faces new competition as BlackRock and State Street have filed to launch their own versions. With $372.5 billion in net assets, QQQ has dominated the market by providing broad exposure to major tech stocks, particularly in the AI sector, while maintaining diversification—no single stock exceeds 5% of its assets.

The entrance of these financial giants into the Nasdaq-100 ETF space could reshape the competitive landscape, potentially impacting QQQ's market share. As these new offerings emerge, investors will have more options for accessing tech-heavy portfolios, which could lead to pricing pressure and affect performance metrics across the sector.

Market professionals should watch for how this increased competition influences investor behavior and ETF flows, as well as any shifts in the performance of existing funds like QQQ amidst the evolving landscape.
