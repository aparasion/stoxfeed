XRP is currently trading near $1.44, caught in a tight range as it approaches a potential breakout point. The price action suggests a "triangle squeeze," with institutional demand gaining traction as spot XRP ETFs report continued inflows and significant exchange outflows reduce selling pressure. This consolidation indicates that while sellers are still active, they are gradually losing control, setting the stage for a decisive price movement.

Market participants are closely monitoring key levels: a breakout above $1.50 could signal a bullish shift, while a drop below $1.39 would indicate bearish sentiment. The current price compression reflects a market that is absorbing supply, with rising participation hinting at a potential trigger for a sharp move. The recent inflows have pushed total institutional positioning above $2.6 billion, supporting the market even as XRP stalls.

Traders should remain vigilant, as the tightening range suggests a significant price movement is imminent, with direction hinging on which key level breaks first.
