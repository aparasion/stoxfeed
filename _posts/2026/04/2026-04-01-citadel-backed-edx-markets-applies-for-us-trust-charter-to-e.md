EDX Markets, a crypto exchange backed by Citadel Securities, Fidelity, and Charles Schwab, has applied for a national trust bank charter from the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency. This move aims to enhance its offerings for institutional clients by providing regulated custody, asset management, and principal trading services, while maintaining a clear separation from its core trading platform.

The application reflects a growing trend among crypto firms seeking regulatory frameworks that mirror traditional financial market structures. As institutional demand for digital asset services increases, EDX's potential approval could position it to attract significant capital from large financial institutions looking for secure, regulated environments to operate in. The competitive landscape is intensifying, with many firms vying for trust charters to establish credibility and safeguard against counterparty risks.

For market professionals, EDX's pursuit of a trust charter underscores the importance of regulatory compliance in attracting institutional investment in crypto markets, highlighting a pivotal shift towards traditional finance principles in the digital asset space.
