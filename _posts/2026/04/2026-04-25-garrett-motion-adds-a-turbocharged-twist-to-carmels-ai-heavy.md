Carmel Capital Management L.L.C. has established a new position in Garrett Motion (GTX), acquiring 314,750 shares valued at approximately $5.87 million during the first quarter of 2026. This new stake, which represents 1.6% of the fund's assets under management, reflects both the purchase and a significant rise in the stock's value, with the position now worth $5.72 million.

Garrett Motion has seen impressive performance, with shares up 129% over the past year, significantly outperforming the S&P 500. The company specializes in turbocharging and electric-boosting technologies for the automotive sector, making it a strategic fit within Carmel's broader portfolio, which already includes key players in the automotive supply chain. This acquisition signals a focus on the industrial and automotive sectors, which are crucial as the market continues to evolve.
