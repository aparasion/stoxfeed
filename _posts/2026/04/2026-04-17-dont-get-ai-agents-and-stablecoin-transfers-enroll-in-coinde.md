CoinDesk is launching its School of Stablecoin and Agentic Commerce at the Consensus 2026 conference in Miami, scheduled for May 5-7. This initiative aims to equip finance and product leaders with practical knowledge and tools to implement stablecoin and AI agent technologies, which are increasingly crucial for enhancing business efficiency. The program features interactive workshops and sessions led by industry experts, focusing on real-world applications such as setting up stablecoin wallets and utilizing AI agents to streamline operations.

As organizations seek to integrate these innovative solutions, the School of Stablecoin and Agentic Commerce addresses a pressing need for actionable insights in a rapidly evolving financial landscape. Participants will gain hands-on experience and strategic guidance, enabling them to leverage these technologies effectively within their teams.

For market professionals, this initiative represents a valuable opportunity to stay ahead of the curve in adopting transformative financial technologies, ensuring they can navigate the complexities of both decentralized finance (DeFi) and traditional finance (TradFi) landscapes.
