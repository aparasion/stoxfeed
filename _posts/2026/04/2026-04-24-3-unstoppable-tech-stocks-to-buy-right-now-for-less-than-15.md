The tech sector continues to offer attractive growth opportunities, particularly among lesser-known stocks priced under $15. While giants like Microsoft and Nvidia dominate headlines, investors may find value in companies such as UiPath (PATH), Gorilla Technology Group (GRRR), and Duos Technologies Group (DUOT). Each of these firms showcases unique strengths that could appeal to savvy investors seeking growth at a lower entry point.

UiPath is capitalizing on its transition to agentic AI, enhancing its automation capabilities and expanding its market reach, evidenced by a 13% revenue increase to $1.61 billion in fiscal 2026. Gorilla Technology is leveraging its AI-driven video analytics and cybersecurity services, reporting a 35.7% revenue growth in 2025. Meanwhile, Duos Technologies is experiencing explosive growth in the rail inspection sector, with revenues soaring 270% in 2025, although it still faces profitability challenges.

For market professionals, these stocks present compelling investment opportunities, particularly UiPath, which is already profitable. However, Duos Technologies may offer the highest potential upside for those willing to embrace risk, given its rapid revenue growth and strategic shift toward recurring revenue models.
