As April unfolds, retirees and those nearing retirement are urged to reassess their Social Security benefits to ensure accuracy and alignment with their financial needs. Key steps include checking Medicare premium deductions for correctness, updating personal information on the SSA's mySocialSecurity page, and confirming that cost-of-living adjustments (COLA) have been accurately applied to benefits.

This proactive approach is crucial for financial stability, particularly as retirees navigate potential changes in income due to life events like retirement or divorce. Ensuring that income-related monthly adjustment amounts (IRMAAs) reflect current circumstances can lead to significant savings, while reviewing tax withholdings can prevent unexpected liabilities. 

Ultimately, April serves as a strategic checkpoint for retirees to align their Social Security benefits with their financial goals, making it an opportune time to adjust withdrawal strategies if necessary. This diligence not only safeguards against potential errors but also enhances overall retirement planning.
