**Market Brief for April 20, 2026**

**What Happened Today**  
U.S. stock markets experienced declines today, with the S&P 500 down 0.41%, the Dow down 0.31%, and the Nasdaq down 0.66%. This downturn was primarily driven by escalating geopolitical tensions surrounding the U.S.-Iran conflict, which raised concerns about oil supply disruptions, leading to a surge in crude oil prices—Brent crude jumped nearly 6% to surpass $90 per barrel. Market participants are closely watching the upcoming earnings reports, particularly from Tesla and ServiceNow, which could significantly influence sentiment in the tech sector.
