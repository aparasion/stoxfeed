Public has launched Generated Assets, an innovative AI-driven tool designed to simplify the creation of hedged portfolios. Investors can input specific prompts, such as "companies benefiting from rising oil prices," and the AI will analyze every US stock to build a custom index tailored to the user's investment thesis. This feature not only streamlines the hedging process but also provides clear reasoning for stock selections, enhancing decision-making with contextual data.

The introduction of Generated Assets comes at a crucial time when market volatility, driven by rising oil prices and geopolitical tensions, necessitates effective risk management strategies. Public's platform allows investors to backtest their ideas against the S&P 500, ensuring that decisions are data-driven rather than speculative. Additionally, the platform offers a comprehensive suite of investment options, including stocks, crypto, and bonds, all while maintaining robust security measures.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is the potential of AI to enhance portfolio management strategies, making it easier to adapt to changing market conditions while providing deeper insights into asset performance.
