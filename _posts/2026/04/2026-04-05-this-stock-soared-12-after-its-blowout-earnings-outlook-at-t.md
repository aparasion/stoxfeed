Chewy (CHWY) surged 12% following a robust fourth-quarter earnings report and an optimistic outlook for 2026, signaling strong growth potential for the pet care company. Management projects revenue growth to accelerate from 8.3% in 2025 to 8.5% in 2026, alongside significant EBITDA margin expansion, which is expected to rise from 5.7% to between 6.6% and 6.8% this year, with long-term goals of exceeding 10%.

The company’s strategic initiatives, including the addition of 10 new Vet Care clinics and an expanded private-label product line, are designed to enhance customer loyalty and drive sales. Chewy's Autoship program, which accounted for 84% of net sales in Q4, further supports stable revenue and operational efficiency. Despite the recent stock uptick, Chewy remains attractively valued at 12 times EBITDA expectations, with anticipated EBITDA growth of 25% to 30% this year.

For market professionals, Chewy presents a compelling investment opportunity, combining strong growth metrics with a favorable valuation, making it a noteworthy candidate for long-term portfolios.
