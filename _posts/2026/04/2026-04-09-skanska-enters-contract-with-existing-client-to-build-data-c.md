Skanska (SKAb.ST) has secured a $75 million contract to construct a data center in Georgia, USA, with completion expected by Q1 2028. This project will contribute to the company’s US order bookings for Q1 2026, reflecting ongoing demand in the data infrastructure sector, which is critical for supporting digital transformation initiatives across various industries.

Additionally, Skanska has signed a significant contract worth 1.1 billion Swedish kronor with Bane NOR for the Stange-Otterstad rail infrastructure project in Norway, set to be included in Nordic order bookings for Q2 2026. These contracts underscore Skanska's robust pipeline and its strategic focus on infrastructure development, which is likely to bolster its revenue streams in the coming years.

For investors, these contracts highlight Skanska's growth potential in the infrastructure market, particularly in technology and transportation sectors, suggesting a positive outlook for the company's future earnings and order backlog.
