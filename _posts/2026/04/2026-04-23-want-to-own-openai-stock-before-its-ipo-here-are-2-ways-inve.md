OpenAI, the creator of ChatGPT, is set to launch its initial public offering (IPO) in Q4 2023, boasting a staggering valuation of $852 billion. The company reported a remarkable 225% revenue increase to $13 billion in 2025, with projections estimating sales will soar to $30 billion in 2026. This rapid growth translates into a hefty valuation of 65 times sales, positioning the IPO as a highly anticipated event in the market.

For investors eager to gain exposure before the IPO, the Ark Venture Fund and Microsoft present two viable options. The Ark Venture Fund, while offering a high-risk, high-reward profile with significant investments in private companies like OpenAI and SpaceX, has achieved a 151% return since its inception. Conversely, Microsoft, which holds a 27% stake in OpenAI valued at approximately $230 billion, offers a more stable investment. Additionally, Microsoft's revenue-sharing agreement with OpenAI could yield up to $300 billion over the next seven years.

In summary, as OpenAI prepares for its IPO, market professionals should consider both the high-risk potential of the Ark Venture Fund and the more stable, growth-oriented investment in Microsoft to capitalize on the generative AI boom.
