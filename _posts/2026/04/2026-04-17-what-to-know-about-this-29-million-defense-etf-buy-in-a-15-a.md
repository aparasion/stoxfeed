True Vision MN LLC has initiated a new position in the iShares Defense Industrials Active ETF (NASDAQ: IDEF), acquiring 87,908 shares valued at $2.88 million in the first quarter of 2026. This investment, while not among the firm’s top five holdings, represents a strategic move to gain exposure in the defense and industrials sectors amidst rising global defense spending and geopolitical tensions, particularly between the U.S. and Iran.

The IDEF ETF, launched in May 2025, has quickly grown to approximately $3.6 billion in assets, focusing on companies like RTX, Lockheed Martin, and Northrop Grumman. With 1.48% of True Vision's $193.90 million in reportable U.S. equity assets allocated to IDEF, this position underscores a targeted approach to capitalize on sector-specific growth while maintaining a diversified portfolio. 

For market professionals, this move highlights the increasing importance of sector-specific ETFs in navigating geopolitical risks and capitalizing on macroeconomic trends, particularly in defense and industrials.
