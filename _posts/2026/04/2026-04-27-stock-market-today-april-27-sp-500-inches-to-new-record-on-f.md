The S&P 500 reached a new high, gaining 0.12% to close at 7,173.91, while the Nasdaq Composite rose 0.20% to 24,887.10, driven by strong performance in the AI sector. In contrast, the Dow Jones Industrial Average slipped 0.13% to 49,167.79, weighed down by geopolitical tensions and concerns over interest rates. Nvidia and Micron Technology were standout performers, buoyed by positive analyst sentiment, while Microsoft’s stock fluctuated after losing exclusive access to OpenAI technology.

The ongoing optimism around AI and data centers is a key driver for market sentiment, even as tensions in the Middle East and rising oil prices create headwinds. Investors are particularly focused on upcoming earnings reports from major tech firms, including several from the "Magnificent Seven."
