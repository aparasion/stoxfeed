Skanska AB has divested its entire 50% stake in I-4 Mobility Partners HoldCo LLC for approximately $75 million, selling the stake to existing partner John Laing Group. This transaction, completed after receiving necessary approvals, will be recorded in Skanska's financials for the second quarter of 2026. I-4 Mobility Partners is pivotal in the development of the I-4 Ultimate highway project in Orlando, Florida, a significant public-private partnership aimed at enhancing traffic flow and safety on a critical 34-kilometre stretch of Interstate 4.

This divestment reflects Skanska's strategic focus on optimizing its portfolio, particularly in the infrastructure sector, which is currently under pressure due to rising costs and supply chain challenges. Following the announcement, Skanska's shares dipped 2.38% to SEK 237.50, indicating market sensitivity to such strategic shifts.

Investors should monitor how this divestment impacts Skanska's future earnings and overall project pipeline, especially in the context of ongoing infrastructure investments in the U.S.
