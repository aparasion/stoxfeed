Spot Bitcoin ETFs have experienced a notable nine-day inflow streak, reflecting a robust demand amid a strengthening Bitcoin market, which is currently trading at $77,516.55—up 10.73% over the past month. Cumulative net inflows for 2026 have reached $58.23 billion, suggesting that investors are adopting a long-term perspective despite Bitcoin still being approximately 35% below its all-time high from early October. This trend indicates a resilient investor base, often referred to as “diamond hands” in crypto circles.

The inflows into Bitcoin ETFs have outpaced previous records, with a recent surge of $1.9 billion, highlighting a shift in investor sentiment toward sustained investment rather than reacting to short-term market fluctuations. Meanwhile, US spot Ether ETFs also showcased strong inflows, although they faced a setback with net outflows of $75.94 million on April 23 after a solid streak.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is the growing confidence among ETF investors in the cryptocurrency space, suggesting a potential shift in market dynamics that could influence future price movements and investment strategies.
