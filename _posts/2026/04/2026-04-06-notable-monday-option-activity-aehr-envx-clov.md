Enovix Corp (ENVX) experienced a surge in options trading today, with 47,349 contracts exchanged, representing about 4.7 million underlying shares—approximately 85% of its average daily volume. Notably, the $7 strike call option expiring on October 16, 2026, saw significant interest, with 11,736 contracts traded, highlighting strong bullish sentiment among traders.

Similarly, Clover Health Investments Corp (CLOV) reported robust options activity, with 45,122 contracts traded, equating to around 4.5 million underlying shares or 80% of its average daily volume. The $2 strike call option expiring on April 10, 2026, attracted particular attention, with 13,076 contracts changing hands, indicating a potential upside outlook from investors.

The heightened options activity in both ENVX and CLOV suggests increased investor interest and potential volatility ahead. Market professionals should monitor these developments closely, as they may signal shifts in sentiment or upcoming strategic moves by institutional players.
