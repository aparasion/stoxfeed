Shares of Nebius Group (NASDAQ: NBIS) surged 8.8% this past week, buoyed by positive analyst sentiment and ongoing developments in the AI infrastructure sector. The stock opened the week with a notable 6.6% gain, closing at $154.56 on Monday, following Bank of America analyst Tal Liani's price target increase from $150 to $175. This new target suggests a potential upside of over 13%, driven by significant contracts from hyperscalers and the company's plans for a new data center in Finland.

The contrasting views among analysts highlight the volatility in investor sentiment surrounding Nebius. While Bank of America is optimistic, Freedom Capital downgraded its stance to "hold" while raising its price target to $154 from $108. This divergence underscores the importance of critical analysis when navigating AI stocks, especially as Nebius prepares to report its first-quarter 2026 financial results.

Investors should remain vigilant and consider upcoming earnings as a key indicator of the company's trajectory amidst varying analyst opinions.
