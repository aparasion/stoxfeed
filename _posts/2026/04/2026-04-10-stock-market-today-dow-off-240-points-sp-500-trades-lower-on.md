U.S. markets faced pressure today, with the Dow Jones Industrial Average dropping 240 points as investor anxiety over escalating tensions between the U.S. and Iran looms ahead of weekend cease-fire discussions. The geopolitical uncertainty is impacting oil prices, which initially saw gains but have since moderated. This volatility could affect sectors sensitive to oil price fluctuations, such as energy and transportation, while also influencing overall market sentiment as traders assess the potential ramifications of the cease-fire talks on global stability.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is to monitor developments closely, as any positive or negative news from the cease-fire negotiations could lead to swift market reactions, particularly in energy stocks and broader indices.
