Sanofi (SNY) is set to release its Q1 2026 earnings on April 23, with analysts projecting a normalized EPS of €1.8 and revenues around €10.23 billion. This announcement follows the recent leadership change, as Belén Garijo takes over from Paul Hudson, which could signal a strategic shift in the company's direction.

The anticipated earnings results are particularly significant given Dupixent's role in driving revenue growth for Sanofi. Investors will be keen to see how this key product contributes to the overall financial performance, especially amid a backdrop of ongoing profitability expectations over the next five years. Any deviation from these projections could impact stock performance and investor sentiment in the biopharmaceutical sector.

Market professionals should closely monitor Sanofi's earnings report for insights into its growth trajectory and the effectiveness of its new leadership, as these factors could influence stock valuations and sector dynamics in the coming months.
