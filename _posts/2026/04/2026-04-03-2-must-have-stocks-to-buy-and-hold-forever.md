Ferrari (RACE) and Ford Motor Company (F) stand out as must-have stocks in the automotive sector, each offering unique advantages for investors. Ferrari showcases exceptional pricing power and operating margins that rival luxury brands, driven by its limited production strategy and high-demand models like the F80. Despite recent guidance that fell short of expectations, the stock presents a compelling entry point for investors looking to capitalize on its strong brand and profitability.

In contrast, Ford appeals to income-focused investors with a robust dividend yield of 5%, supported by a commitment to return 40% to 50% of annual free cash flow to shareholders. The company has a history of supplemental dividends, enhancing its attractiveness, especially as it addresses previous quality issues and pivots its EV strategy toward profitability.

Ultimately, both stocks represent strong long-term holdings: Ferrari for its unmatched margins and brand loyalty, and Ford for its stable dividends and potential for recovery in earnings.
