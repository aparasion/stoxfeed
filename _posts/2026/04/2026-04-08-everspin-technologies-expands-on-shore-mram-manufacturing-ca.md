Everspin Technologies Inc. (NASDAQ: MRAM) has forged a significant 10-year strategic manufacturing agreement with Microchip Technology to enhance its production capacity for Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) and Tunnel Magnetoresistive (TMR) sensor products. This partnership will establish a new MRAM production line at Microchip's Oregon facility, allowing Everspin to maintain ownership of its intellectual property while benefiting from Microchip's extensive manufacturing capabilities.

This collaboration is poised to impact the semiconductor sector by addressing the increasing demand for MRAM solutions, particularly as customers seek more reliable supply chains and advanced technologies. Everspin's expansion will not only increase wafer capacity but also ensure continuity of supply well into the next decade, supporting its growth plans and ongoing R&D initiatives.

Market professionals should note that Everspin's strategic move positions the company to capitalize on the growing adoption of MRAM technology, with first shipments expected in the latter half of 2027, potentially enhancing its competitive edge in the memory solutions market.
