On February 12, 2026, Latin Lawyer convened a roundtable featuring leading M&A partners to discuss the evolving landscape of mergers and acquisitions across Latin America. Chaired by Paola Lozano from Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP, the session focused on key developments in Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Mexico, and Venezuela, while also incorporating insights from the U.S. and Spain.

This dialogue is particularly significant as it highlights the complexities and opportunities within the Latin American M&A market, which has seen increased activity amid economic fluctuations and regulatory changes. The insights shared by industry leaders can provide valuable context for investors and analysts monitoring cross-border transactions and sector-specific trends in these emerging markets.

For market professionals, understanding these dynamics is crucial, as they can influence investment strategies and portfolio allocations in Latin America, especially in sectors poised for growth in the wake of these developments.
