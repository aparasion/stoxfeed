Jersey Mike's has confidentially filed for an initial public offering (IPO) slated for April 20, 2026, aiming for an initial valuation of approximately $12 billion. However, details remain sparse, as the company has not disclosed financial specifics or a definitive IPO date. Market conditions could influence the timing, with sources suggesting a potential launch in the third quarter of this year.

The anticipated valuation reflects a steep price-to-sales multiple of 38.7, significantly higher than the restaurant industry's average of 2.5. This premium valuation underscores Wall Street's enthusiasm, especially given Jersey Mike's growth trajectory, with plans to expand its U.S. footprint and enter the Canadian market. Notably, investors can gain indirect exposure through Blackstone, which holds a majority stake in Jersey Mike's.

As Jersey Mike's moves toward its S-1 filing with the SEC, market professionals should monitor this process closely, as it will provide critical financial data and insights into the company’s growth strategy, potentially positioning it among the standout IPOs of 2026.
