The S&P 500 rose 1.03% to 7,137.12, the Nasdaq Composite surged 1.64% to a record high of 24,657.57, and the Dow Jones Industrial Average gained 0.69% to 49,490.02, driven by a broad relief rally. Mega-cap tech stocks were pivotal, with Alphabet advancing 2% following significant announcements, including new AI partnerships and a $1 billion deal with Merck. Meanwhile, Coinbase climbed 5% amid a bullish crypto market, and CrowdStrike rose 4% after a favorable analyst upgrade.

This rally was largely fueled by easing geopolitical tensions, particularly news of an extended ceasefire in the Iran War, which contributed to a risk-on sentiment across the markets. The tech sector's performance, especially Alphabet's strategic moves in AI and cloud partnerships, underscores the ongoing investor interest in growth-oriented stocks.

For market professionals, today’s developments highlight the importance of monitoring geopolitical events and their influence on market sentiment, as well as the potential for tech stocks like Alphabet to drive future performance.
