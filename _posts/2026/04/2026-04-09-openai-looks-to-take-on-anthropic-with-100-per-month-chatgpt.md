OpenAI has introduced a new Pro ChatGPT tier, priced at $100 per month, which significantly boosts usage limits for its Codex coding assistant. This move aims to enhance its competitive edge against Anthropic's Claude Code, which has gained traction since its public launch in May 2025. The Pro tier offers five times the Codex usage compared to the $20 Plus plan, catering to users engaged in more intensive coding sessions.

This development is crucial for the tech sector, particularly in software development, as the demand for AI-driven coding tools continues to surge. OpenAI's Codex, which automates tasks and fixes bugs, reported a run-rate revenue exceeding $2.5 billion in February, reflecting a remarkable growth of over 100% since early 2026. With three million weekly users and a goal of reaching 10 million, OpenAI is positioning itself to capture a larger share of the burgeoning AI coding market.

For market professionals, the introduction of the Pro tier signals a potential shift in revenue streams for AI companies, highlighting the increasing monetization of advanced coding tools and the competitive dynamics in the AI space.
