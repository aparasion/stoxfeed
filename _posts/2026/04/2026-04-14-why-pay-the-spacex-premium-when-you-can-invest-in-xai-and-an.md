The ETF’s structure combines public equities with private market access, offering a unique opportunity to capture growth from both established giants and emerging innovators.

Ultimately, AGIX presents a compelling case for investors seeking long-term growth in AI while minimizing risks tied to individual companies. This strategic approach not only sidesteps the speculative frenzy surrounding SpaceX but also positions investors to benefit from the broader technological advancements shaping the future of the space economy.
