Realty Income (O 1.17%) continues to showcase its strength as a reliable investment, boasting an annualized total return of 13.3% since its 1994 public listing, surpassing the S&P 500's 11.1% return. This performance, bolstered by its high-yielding monthly dividends, positions Realty Income as a compelling option for investors seeking stable real estate exposure.

While a $100,000 investment today is unlikely to reach the $1 million mark in a decade—requiring an unrealistic 26% annualized return—Realty Income remains a solid choice for long-term wealth accumulation. The REIT's consistent dividend growth, with a 4.2% annualized increase over the past three decades, offers a projected monthly income of over $420, which could rise substantially as dividends increase. 

For market professionals, Realty Income represents a strategic investment in a diversified property portfolio with a strong balance sheet, making it a dependable source of income and capital appreciation over time, even if it doesn't produce millionaire-level returns in the short term.
