This differentiation is crucial as it impacts traders' ability to execute timely trades and manage costs effectively.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is that choosing the right broker can significantly enhance short selling strategies. As the landscape becomes increasingly competitive, understanding broker capabilities is essential for optimizing trading performance and minimizing costs.
