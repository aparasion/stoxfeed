Asset Advisors Investment Management, LLC significantly increased its stake in the iShares iBonds Dec 2029 Term Treasury ETF (IBTJ), acquiring an additional 193,119 shares valued at approximately $4.24 million during Q1 2026. This move raised the firm’s total holdings in IBTJ to 1,238,117 shares, which now constitute 2.58% of its reportable assets under management. As of April 15, 2026, IBTJ shares were priced at $21.83, reflecting a 4.4% increase over the past year, with an annualized dividend yield of 3.8%.

The decision to bolster its position in IBTJ underscores a strategic focus on U.S. Treasury bonds maturing in 2029, offering a defined maturity profile that can appeal to investors seeking stability amid market volatility. This ETF’s structure, which mimics a laddered bond portfolio, allows for predictable cash flows and reduces sensitivity to interest rate fluctuations as it approaches maturity.

For market professionals, the increased investment in IBTJ signals confidence in the ETF's ability to provide reliable income and principal protection, making it a compelling option for those looking to navigate uncertain economic conditions.
