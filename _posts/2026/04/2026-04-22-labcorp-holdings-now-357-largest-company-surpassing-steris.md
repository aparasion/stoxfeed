Labcorp Holdings Inc (LH) has reached a market capitalization of $22.53 billion, surpassing STERIS plc (STE) at $21.54 billion. This distinction highlights the importance of market cap as a key metric for investors, providing a more accurate comparison of company value than stock price alone. Understanding market capitalization allows investors to gauge a company's size relative to its peers, influencing which mutual funds and ETFs may include the stock in their portfolios.

Market cap also plays a critical role in determining a company's placement within indices like the S&P 500. For instance, funds focused on large-cap stocks typically target companies with market caps above $10 billion, while the S&P MidCap index emphasizes smaller firms, which can outperform larger counterparts in favorable conditions. As of the latest trading session, LH has seen a slight decline of 0.2%, while STE has risen by 1.9%, reflecting investor sentiment and market dynamics.

For market professionals, keeping an eye on market capitalization trends not only aids in stock selection but also informs broader investment strategies and sector allocations.
