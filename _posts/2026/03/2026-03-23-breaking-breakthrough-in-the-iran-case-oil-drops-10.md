President Trump has ordered the Pentagon to delay military strikes on Iranian energy infrastructure for five days, following productive discussions between the U.S. and Iran. This announcement, shared via his social media account, has triggered immediate market reactions, with the dollar depreciating, oil prices declining, and both stocks and gold experiencing upward momentum.

The market's response reflects heightened investor sentiment surrounding geopolitical stability. The weakening dollar and falling oil prices suggest a potential easing of tensions that could lead to improved economic conditions. Meanwhile, the rise in equities and gold indicates a shift towards safer assets as traders reassess risk exposure amid evolving geopolitical dynamics.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is the potential for volatility in energy and commodity markets as diplomatic developments unfold. Investors should remain vigilant, as any further announcements could significantly impact asset valuations and trading strategies.
