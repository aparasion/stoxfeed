ASML Holdings (ASML) has surged 79.5% over the past year, significantly outperforming the tech sector's 26.8% gain and the Nasdaq Composite's 22.4% increase. Analysts project ASML's earnings per share will rise from $29.69 in 2026 to $37.51 in 2027, yet the stock trades at a high valuation of 35.1 times projected earnings for 2027. This premium reflects ASML's critical role in the semiconductor industry, particularly in the production of AI chips, where its advanced extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography technology offers a competitive edge.

The demand for ASML's cutting-edge EUV machines is driven by major tech players investing in AI data centers, which in turn boosts the need for sophisticated chip manufacturing capabilities. With EUV systems accounting for over half of ASML's net bookings in Q4 2025, the company is positioned for sustained growth as the rollout of high-NA EUV technology continues.

For market professionals, ASML represents a compelling investment opportunity, particularly for those with a long-term outlook. Despite its high valuation, the company's unique technology and strong market position suggest it could outperform major indexes and potentially reach a $1 trillion market cap in the future.
