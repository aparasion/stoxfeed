German IT services provider adesso SE (ADSGF, ADN1.DE) reported a substantial increase in profit for fiscal 2025, with consolidated earnings soaring to €17.46 million from €4.15 million the previous year. The company also raised its dividend to €0.78 per share, up from €0.75, and provided an optimistic outlook for fiscal 2026, projecting further growth in both EBITDA and sales revenues.

This strong performance comes despite challenging economic conditions, with sales revenues rising 14% year-over-year to €1.47 billion. Domestic sales were particularly robust, increasing 15% to €1.23 billion, while foreign revenues also saw a healthy 7% growth. The company’s EBITDA margin improved to 8.4%, reflecting operational efficiency and effective cost management.

For market professionals, adesso's positive outlook and increased dividend signal confidence in sustained demand for its digitalization services, making it a notable player to watch in the IT services sector as it navigates the evolving economic landscape.
