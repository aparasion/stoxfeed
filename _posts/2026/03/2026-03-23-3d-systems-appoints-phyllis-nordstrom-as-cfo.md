3D Systems Corporation (DDD) has appointed Phyllis Nordstrom as executive vice president and chief financial officer, effective March 23, 2026. Nordstrom, who has been the interim CFO since August 2025, will also retain her role as chief administrative officer. With over 25 years of experience in finance and risk management, her leadership is expected to guide the company through its ongoing financial challenges.

The announcement comes at a time when 3D Systems shares are struggling, having closed at $1.93, down 5.39% on Friday. Investors may view Nordstrom’s permanent appointment as a stabilizing factor, especially given her extensive background in both public companies and accounting. This change could influence market sentiment and investor confidence in the firm’s strategic direction.

Market professionals should monitor how Nordstrom’s appointment impacts 3D Systems’ financial performance and operational strategy, particularly as the company seeks to regain momentum in a competitive sector.
