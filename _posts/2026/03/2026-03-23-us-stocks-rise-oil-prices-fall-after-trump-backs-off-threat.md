Investigators will scrutinize air traffic controller records, including time cards and sign-in sheets, to assess staffing levels during the incident. This investigation comes amid concerns about outdated intelligence that may have contributed to the attack, as indicated by a U.S. official.

The implications of this incident could ripple through the aviation sector, particularly affecting airline and defense stocks.
