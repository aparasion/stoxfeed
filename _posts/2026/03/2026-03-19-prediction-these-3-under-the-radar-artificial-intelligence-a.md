Three undervalued artificial intelligence (AI) stocks are gaining attention for their potential to deliver significant returns this year. SoundHound AI (NASDAQ: SOUN) has seen its stock plummet nearly 70% from last fall's highs, yet it boasts strong revenue growth—doubling last year and surging 59% in Q4. With its innovative voice technology and a focus on customer service AI, SoundHound is well-positioned for a rebound.

UiPath (NYSE: PATH) is down over 40% but trades at attractive forward multiples of 3.6 for price-to-sales and 15 for price-to-earnings. The company is transitioning into an agentic AI orchestration platform, with promising growth in annual recurring revenue and a solid strategy for managing AI agents. This could drive significant revenue acceleration.

Lastly, GitLab (NASDAQ: GTLB) is trading nearly 60% lower than its recent highs, with a forward price-to-sales multiple of 3.5. Despite conservative growth guidance, new pricing models and product features may unlock revenue potential.
