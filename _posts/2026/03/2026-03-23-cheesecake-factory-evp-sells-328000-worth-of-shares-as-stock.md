Scarlett May, EVP and General Counsel of The Cheesecake Factory (NASDAQ: CAKE), recently executed an open-market sale of 5,206 shares valued at approximately $328,000, as disclosed in an SEC Form 4 filing. This transaction is notably smaller than her average sell transaction of 18,800 shares over the past year, representing 16.84% of her direct holdings, which now stand at 25,715 shares.

This insider sale comes amid a backdrop of fluctuating stock performance for The Cheesecake Factory, which has seen a 13% increase in 2026 but faced a 10% decline in March. Investors should note that May also holds 31,734 restricted shares worth around $1.81 million, contingent on performance and time-based vesting conditions. The company has acknowledged potential risks to consumer demand from health concerns related to food safety, which could influence future stock performance.

Market professionals should monitor these insider transactions and the broader implications of consumer sentiment on The Cheesecake Factory's stock trajectory throughout the year.
