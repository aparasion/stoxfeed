**Market Brief for March 30, 2026**

**What Happened Today**  
Today, the U.S. stock market exhibited mixed performance amid escalating geopolitical tensions in the Middle East, particularly related to the ongoing Iran conflict. The S&P 500 index saw a slight uptick of 0.58%, buoyed by falling Treasury yields, while the Dow Jones Industrial Average rose as well, reflecting a cautious recovery from last week's declines. However, sectors such as technology faced pressure, with notable declines in chip stocks like Micron Technology, which fell over 5.6%. This spike in oil prices has led to increased scrutiny on companies reliant on low fuel costs, such as budget airlines. Investor sentiment remains cautious as consumer confidence wanes amid rising inflation concerns.
