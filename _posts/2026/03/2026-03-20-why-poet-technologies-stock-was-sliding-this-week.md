Poet Technologies (NASDAQ: POET) has faced a challenging week on the stock exchange, with shares down nearly 9% as investors shift focus toward companies with tangible earnings. Despite announcing two strategic partnerships aimed at advancing its optical communication technology, the market's reaction reflects skepticism about Poet's current revenue-generating capabilities, labeling it more of a "story stock."

The collaborations with Liteon Technology and Lessengers highlight Poet's commitment to developing innovative optical modules, particularly for AI applications. However, the lack of disclosed financial details in these agreements raises concerns about immediate profitability, leaving investors wary. As the company continues to operate in a promising tech segment, its ability to translate these partnerships into revenue remains uncertain.
