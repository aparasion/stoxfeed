US stock futures are continuing their upward momentum as investors anticipate the Federal Reserve's upcoming rate decision. The market's optimism is fueled by recent economic data suggesting a resilient economy, which has led to speculation that the Fed may pause its rate hikes. Major indices are reflecting this positive sentiment, with futures for the S&P 500 and Dow Jones Industrial Average showing significant gains.

This rally matters to financial markets as it signals investor confidence in economic stability, potentially influencing sector performances, particularly in interest-sensitive areas like real estate and utilities. A pause or cut in rates could also bolster consumer spending and corporate earnings, further driving stock prices higher.
