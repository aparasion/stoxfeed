Clearwater is also in the spotlight for a pending $8.4 billion acquisition, with an offer of $24.55 per share, indicating strong investor confidence.

The company’s robust business model, with 98% of revenue from recurring subscriptions, positions it well in the burgeoning connected vehicle market, particularly in emerging regions where digital fleet management is still evolving.

Pagaya Technologies (PGY) achieved its first profitable year in 2025, with revenue hitting $1.3 billion and adjusted EBITDA soaring 76%. Its capital-light model allows for rapid scaling and high margins, making it an attractive opportunity given its valuation relative to revenue.
