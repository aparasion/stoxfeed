In a market characterized by uncertainty, three stocks stand out for their resilience and essential services: Motorola Solutions (MSI), Rollins (ROL), and Murphy USA (MUSA). Each of these companies provides critical products and services that remain in demand regardless of economic conditions, making them attractive options for risk-averse investors.

Motorola Solutions generates about 75% of its revenue from public safety and defense, ensuring consistent demand through long-term contracts. With an 8% sales growth forecast for 2025 and a solid free cash flow growth rate, it trades at 30 times FCF. Rollins, the leading pest control provider, has seen impressive annual sales and cash flow growth, though it carries a premium valuation at 40 times FCF. Murphy USA, strategically located near Walmart stores, benefits from a steady customer base and is committed to share buybacks and dividends, making it a strong contender for investors seeking stability.

For market professionals, these stocks represent a compelling mix of growth potential and defensive qualities, particularly in turbulent times.
