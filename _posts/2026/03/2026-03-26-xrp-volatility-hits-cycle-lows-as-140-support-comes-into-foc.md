XRP is currently experiencing its lowest volatility since January, trading just above the critical $1.40 support level. This tight range, characterized by repeated rejections near $1.43 and persistent buying pressure at $1.40, suggests that a significant breakout is imminent. Traders are closely monitoring this compression phase, as a decisive move either above resistance or below support could trigger a sharp price shift.

The implications for financial markets are noteworthy. Should XRP hold above $1.40, a rebound toward $1.43–$1.45 could materialize, potentially revitalizing interest in the cryptocurrency sector. Conversely, a clean break below $1.40 would open the door to further declines, possibly targeting $1.35. With volume expected to confirm the next trend, market participants should remain vigilant as the situation develops.

In summary, the $1.40 level is pivotal for XRP, and its behavior in the coming sessions will likely dictate the direction of the next significant price movement.
