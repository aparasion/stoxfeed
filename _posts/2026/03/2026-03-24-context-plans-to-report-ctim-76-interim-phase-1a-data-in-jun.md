Context Therapeutics Inc. (CNTX) reported a widened net loss of $36.13 million for the full year 2025, compared to a loss of $26.73 million in 2024. Despite the financial setback, the clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company is advancing its investigational pipeline, focusing on T cell-engaging bispecific antibodies for solid tumors. Key milestones include ongoing Phase 1 trials for CTIM-76 and CT-95, with interim data presentations scheduled for mid-2026 and September 2026, respectively. Additionally, CT-202 is set to enter its Phase 1 trial in Q3 2026.

The company’s cash reserves of $66 million are projected to sustain operations through mid-2027, allowing for continued investment in its clinical programs. As of the latest trading session, CNTX shares closed at $3.02, reflecting a 4.86% gain, indicating market optimism around its clinical developments.

For investors, the upcoming clinical updates and the cash runway provide a critical backdrop for potential stock performance, making CNTX a stock to watch in the biotech sector.
