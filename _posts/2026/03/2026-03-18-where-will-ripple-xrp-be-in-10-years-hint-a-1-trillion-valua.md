Ripple's XRP is positioning itself for significant growth, with CEO Brad Garlinghouse projecting a potential market cap of $1 trillion over the next decade. Despite a market cap drop from $200 billion to nearly $100 billion amid broader crypto market declines, Ripple's strategy is shifting towards building a robust ecosystem rather than directly replacing legacy payment systems like SWIFT.

This ecosystem approach aims to foster a network of decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, payment solutions, and partnerships that enhance the utility of the XRP ledger. By creating a comprehensive infrastructure that includes wallets, developer tools, and liquidity providers, Ripple seeks to drive adoption and value across various endpoints. Additionally, the tokenization of real-world assets presents a transformative opportunity, potentially establishing Ripple as a central player in the burgeoning "Internet of Value."
