Apple CEO Tim Cook addressed retirement rumors during a "Good Morning America" interview, affirming his commitment to the company he has led for nearly three decades. His comments come amid a series of executive departures that have raised questions about Apple's leadership and its ability to compete in the rapidly evolving AI landscape. Notably, the company is gearing up for significant product launches, including a foldable iPhone and AI glasses, while still working to enhance its Siri experience.

This reassurance from Cook is crucial as Apple approaches its 50th anniversary and navigates a pivotal moment in its history. Analysts like Walter Piecyk have expressed concerns that Apple risks losing ground in AI to competitors like Google, especially after its recent partnership to integrate AI features powered by Google’s Gemini. The stock remains near all-time highs, but Cook's leadership style and strategic decisions will be closely scrutinized as the company transitions into this new technological era.
