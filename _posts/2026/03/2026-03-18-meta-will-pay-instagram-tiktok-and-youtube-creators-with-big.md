Meta has launched the Creator Fast Track program, targeting top creators from TikTok and YouTube to enhance content on Facebook. This initiative guarantees monthly payments of $1,000 for creators with at least 100,000 followers and $3,000 for those exceeding one million, aiming to address the challenges creators face when starting on the platform. While these payments last for three months, creators will gain ongoing access to Facebook's Content Monetization program and a lasting boost in reach.

This move comes as Meta seeks to reclaim its position in the creator economy, having paid nearly $3 billion to creators in 2025 alone. The program is designed to attract established influencers to produce original content on Facebook, a platform that has struggled to retain creators amid the popularity of TikTok and YouTube. By shifting from a revenue share model to one based on engagement, Meta hopes to create a more appealing environment for creators.
