The Motley Fool's 2026 Best Places to Retire report highlights key states for retirees, revealing Florida, Pennsylvania, and Texas as top contenders based on factors like healthcare, amenities, and cost of living. Florida stands out with cities like Fort Lauderdale and St. Augustine, attracting retirees with their vibrant social scenes and warm climates, despite challenges like limited healthcare access and hurricane risks. 

Pennsylvania offers surprising options, with Philadelphia ranking sixth nationally, providing excellent healthcare and a rich historical backdrop, though harsh winters may deter some. Texas features five cities in the top 20, including Austin and Dallas, known for affordability and entertainment, though property taxes can vary significantly.
