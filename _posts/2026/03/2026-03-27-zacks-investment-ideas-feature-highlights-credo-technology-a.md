Credo Technology Group (CRDO) and Vertiv (VRT) are emerging as key players in the booming AI data center market, with both companies experiencing significant stock performance over the past three years—gaining over 1,000%. Credo specializes in high-speed connectivity components essential for AI workloads, while Vertiv provides critical power and cooling systems for AI data centers. Despite a 30% decline in CRDO's stock this year, analysts suggest it remains a strong buy due to ongoing demand for its products. Conversely, VRT has seen a 60% rise in stock value this year, driven by the increasing need for advanced cooling solutions.

The implications for investors are noteworthy. Credo's projected sales growth of 204% in fiscal 2026 and Vertiv's expected 34% revenue increase highlight their potential to capitalize on the surging demand for AI infrastructure. Both companies are trading at reasonable forward P/E multiples, making them attractive options for those looking to invest in the AI sector.

In summary, both CRDO and VRT present compelling investment opportunities as they play pivotal roles in the AI data center ecosystem, with strong earnings growth and favorable valuations supporting their market positions.
