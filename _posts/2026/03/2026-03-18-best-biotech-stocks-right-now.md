Biotech stocks are gaining renewed attention as the global economy rebounds from the pandemic, with major players like AbbVie, Johnson & Johnson, and Pfizer leading the charge in developing innovative therapies. AbbVie’s acquisition of Allergan has diversified its portfolio, while Johnson & Johnson continues to leverage its extensive drug and device divisions, which account for 80% of its sales. Pfizer remains a key player with its wide-ranging therapeutic offerings, including COVID-19 treatments.

This sector's growth potential is closely tied to the approval of new drugs, which can significantly boost stock values overnight. As the demand for effective treatments rises, especially in oncology and immunology, investors are increasingly looking to biotech as a lucrative opportunity. Companies focused on rare diseases may also see heightened interest due to their unique market positions.
