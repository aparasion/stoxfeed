SoundHound AI (SOUN) has seen dramatic fluctuations since its SPAC debut in April 2022, with a staggering 836% rise in 2024 followed by a sharp decline, including a 24% drop in 2026. Despite this volatility, the company is positioned to capitalize on the booming voice AI market, which is projected to grow from $11.6 billion in 2024 to $41.4 billion by 2030, at a CAGR of 24%. SoundHound's revenue nearly doubled to $169 million in 2025, driven by over 100 new deals across diverse sectors, indicating strong demand for its AI solutions.

Investors should note that SoundHound's growth trajectory is outpacing the overall conversational AI market, with projected revenues of $225 million to $260 million for 2026. While its sales multiple of 17.7 is higher than the tech sector average, this premium is somewhat justified given its rapid growth.
