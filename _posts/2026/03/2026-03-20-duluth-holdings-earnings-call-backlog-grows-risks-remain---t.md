Duluth Holdings reported a notable increase in its backlog during the latest earnings call, signaling potential growth opportunities ahead. The company highlighted that while demand for its outdoor apparel remains strong, it is facing challenges related to supply chain disruptions and rising costs, which could impact future profitability.

This backlog growth is significant for investors as it suggests a positive outlook for revenue in the coming quarters, despite the existing risks. The apparel sector, particularly outdoor brands, is experiencing heightened competition, making effective management of these challenges crucial for maintaining market share.
