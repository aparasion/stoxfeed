In light of ongoing Middle East conflicts, investors are navigating heightened uncertainty that could impact high-flying AI stocks. This environment may prompt a shift towards dividend-paying consumer stocks, which typically offer stability and lower volatility. Clorox (CLX), currently trading at $17 times earnings, presents an attractive opportunity, particularly for those looking to invest $5,000.

Clorox boasts a robust dividend yield of approximately 4.7%, surpassing the 10-year Treasury yield of 4.4%. Despite a challenging period marked by a cyberattack and a 10% decline in sales for the first half of fiscal 2026, analysts predict a gradual recovery, with sales expected to rebound by 5% in fiscal 2027. The company’s strong free cash flow of $778 million comfortably covers its $602 million dividend obligations, reinforcing its commitment to maintaining dividend increases.

For investors seeking reliable income amid market volatility, Clorox stands out as a compelling option. Its combination of a solid dividend and potential for growth could make it a strategic addition to portfolios in the current economic climate.
