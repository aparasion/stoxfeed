H World Group Limited (HTHT) reported a strong fourth-quarter performance, posting a Non-GAAP EPS of $0.53, surpassing analyst expectations by $0.18. The company also achieved revenue of $933 million, which reflects an 8.3% year-over-year increase and beats forecasts by $5.87 million. As of December 31, 2025, H World Group operated 12,858 hotels and 1,264,419 rooms, indicating robust operational growth.

This earnings report is significant for investors as it highlights H World Group's resilience in the hospitality sector amid ongoing economic fluctuations. The solid revenue growth and EPS beat suggest effective management strategies and a potential for continued market share expansion. The increase in hotel turnover further underscores the company's operational strength, which could positively influence future earnings projections.
