Investors are increasingly eyeing the artificial intelligence (AI) sector for potential "millionaire-maker" stocks, with SoundHound AI (SOUN) and Nebius (NBIS) emerging as notable contenders. SoundHound AI specializes in AI-powered audio recognition software, currently focused on expanding its client base beyond restaurant drive-thrus into sectors like insurance and healthcare. Meanwhile, Nebius is carving out a niche as an AI-first cloud computing provider, with projected revenue growth from $1.25 billion in 2025 to as much as $9 billion this year, driven by rising demand for AI computing power.

While both companies show promise, they come with inherent risks, making them suitable for only a small portion of a diversified portfolio. The potential for substantial returns exists, but investors should temper expectations regarding achieving millionaire status solely through these stocks.
