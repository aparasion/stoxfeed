Marvell Technology Inc (MRVL) experienced a surge in options trading today, with 160,810 contracts exchanged, equating to about 16.1 million underlying shares—an impressive 72% of its average daily volume. Notably, the $100 strike call option expiring on March 27, 2026, accounted for a significant portion of this activity, with 34,867 contracts traded, indicating strong bullish sentiment among traders.

In contrast, Corcept Therapeutics Inc (CORT) also saw robust options activity, with 10,145 contracts traded, representing approximately 71.8% of its average daily volume. The $50 strike call option expiring on April 17, 2026, was particularly active, with 2,322 contracts exchanged, suggesting heightened investor interest in potential upside.

For market professionals, the elevated options volume in both MRVL and CORT signals notable investor engagement and could indicate expectations of significant price movements ahead. Monitoring these trends may provide insights into broader market sentiment and sector dynamics.
