CVS Health Corporation (CVS) has announced a quarterly dividend of $0.665 per share, consistent with previous distributions. This dividend will be payable on May 4, with shareholders of record as of April 23, and an ex-dividend date also set for April 23. 

This announcement comes at a pivotal time for CVS, as the company faces scrutiny over Medicare fraud allegations, leading to a $117.7 million settlement. Additionally, concerns regarding margin compression in its Aetna division may limit growth potential in the near term. These factors could influence investor sentiment and stock performance, particularly as CVS navigates its financial commitments and operational challenges.
