DKSH Holding AG has announced its agreement to acquire AIC Ingredients Sdn Bhd, a move aimed at bolstering its presence in Southeast Asia's bakery-focused functional ingredients market. While financial terms remain undisclosed, the acquisition is anticipated to close in the second quarter of 2026, enhancing DKSH's capabilities in food blending and manufacturing under its ekpi brand.

This strategic acquisition positions DKSH to strengthen its regional formulation and manufacturing capabilities, establishing a third pillar for its ekpi offerings in Malaysia. AIC Ingredients, founded in 1998, brings specialized expertise in developing functional blends for the bakery industry, which could lead to increased market share and improved service offerings in a growing sector.

For market professionals, this acquisition signals DKSH's commitment to expanding its operational footprint in Southeast Asia, potentially impacting its stock performance and long-term growth strategy as it leverages AIC's established market position and technical expertise.
