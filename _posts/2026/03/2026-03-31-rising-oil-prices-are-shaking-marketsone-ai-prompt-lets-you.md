Public has launched a new feature called Generated Assets, leveraging AI to help investors build customized hedged portfolios more efficiently. By simply entering a prompt related to their investment thesis—such as "companies benefiting from rising oil prices"—investors can receive tailored stock recommendations. The platform evaluates all U.S. stocks to create a custom index, providing clarity on each stock's inclusion and allowing users to backtest their strategies against the S&P 500.

This innovation is particularly relevant in today’s volatile market environment, where rising oil prices and geopolitical tensions can complicate risk management. Generated Assets not only streamlines the portfolio construction process but also enhances decision-making by providing context and rationale behind stock selections. 

For market professionals, Public's integration of AI tools like Alpha, which offers real-time data analysis and performance summaries, represents a significant advancement in investment technology. This could lead to more informed trading strategies and improved portfolio outcomes, making it a noteworthy development in the financial services landscape.
