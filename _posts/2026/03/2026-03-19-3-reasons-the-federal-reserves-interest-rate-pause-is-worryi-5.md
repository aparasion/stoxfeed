The closure poses risks not just to oil prices but also to broader market stability, as fears of supply disruptions could lead to volatility in energy stocks.

The uncertainty surrounding U.S.-Iran relations could lead to increased risk premiums on oil futures, affecting sectors reliant on stable energy prices. Investors should closely monitor developments, as any prolonged closure could exacerbate inflationary pressures and impact global economic growth.
