XRP is currently testing the $1.33 support level as rising leverage and weak price action create a precarious market setup. Despite a slight dip over the past 24 hours, traders are increasingly bullish, as indicated by soaring funding rates and liquidations. However, repeated rejections near the $1.35 to $1.36 range suggest that sellers still dominate the market, leaving XRP in a fragile state.

The implications for the financial markets are significant. If XRP breaks below the $1.33 support, it could quickly slide to $1.30, intensifying bearish sentiment. Conversely, a sustained move above $1.35 to $1.36 is necessary to shift momentum and signal a potential recovery. The current environment indicates a classic tension setup, where increasing bullish positioning is not translating into upward price movement, raising the risk of a sharper decline.

Traders should closely monitor the $1.33 support; a breach could accelerate selling pressure, while reclaiming the $1.35-$1.36 range is critical for a bullish reversal.
