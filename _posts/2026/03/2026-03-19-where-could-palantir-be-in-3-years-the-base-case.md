Palantir Technologies (PLTR) is positioning itself as a key player in the enterprise AI landscape, transitioning from a government-centric model to a commercial powerhouse. With a staggering 109% year-over-year growth in U.S. commercial revenue in 2025, the company is rapidly expanding its footprint across various sectors, including healthcare and manufacturing, as organizations increasingly adopt its AI-driven platforms.

This shift not only highlights Palantir's potential to become a major enterprise AI platform but also underscores the stickiness of its software solutions. As companies integrate Palantir’s systems into their workflows, the challenge of switching providers enhances customer retention, positioning Palantir as essential infrastructure in the operationalization of AI.
