Ethereum, XRP, and Chainlink are highlighted as three cryptocurrencies with significant real-world utility, making them potential investment opportunities. Ethereum, with a market cap of $260 billion, serves as the backbone for decentralized applications and finance, with Ether required for transactions and services on its blockchain. XRP, known as the "banker's coin," facilitates rapid and cost-effective cross-border payments, gaining traction among financial institutions, including a partnership with Western Union. Chainlink, a decentralized oracle network, plays a crucial role in providing real-time data for smart contracts and asset tokenization.

Despite their utility, all three cryptocurrencies have seen declines of over 20% in 2026, raising questions about their future performance.
