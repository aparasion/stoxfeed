Bitcoin is experiencing a notable rebound today, buoyed by a positive equity market opening following a turbulent weekend. The resurgence in BTC is partly driven by renewed buying activity from Bitcoin ETFs, which have accumulated the cryptocurrency for nearly ten consecutive sessions. As geopolitical tensions in the Middle East appear to ease, traders are increasingly optimistic about normalizing shipping traffic through the Strait of Hormuz, reducing concerns over oil supply disruptions.

This shift in focus is benefiting Bitcoin, which has outperformed gold and is approaching a critical resistance level between $74,000 and $75,000. This zone is significant due to dealer gamma positioning in the options market; a breakout above this level could trigger additional buying pressure from funds, while a rejection could lead to a significant pullback below $60,000, reminiscent of previous market corrections.
