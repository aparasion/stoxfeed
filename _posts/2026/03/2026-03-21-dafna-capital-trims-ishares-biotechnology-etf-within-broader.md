DAFNA Capital Management LLC has trimmed its stake in the iShares Biotechnology ETF (IBB) by 34,405 shares during Q4 2025, resulting in a $3.31 million reduction in the position's value. Following this sale, IBB now represents 2.67% of DAFNA's reportable assets under management. The ETF, which tracks the NASDAQ Biotechnology Index, has seen a price increase of 27.2% over the past year, outperforming the S&P 500 by 15.84 percentage points.

This reduction in stake could signal a shift in sentiment towards biotech investments, which are often sensitive to interest rates and market risk appetite. The iShares Biotechnology ETF focuses on large, established companies, offering more predictable earnings compared to equal-weighted biotech funds. However, its performance remains closely tied to the earnings and developments of its largest holdings, making it crucial for investors to monitor the health of these major players.

Investors should note that while IBB provides exposure to established biotech firms, its top-heavy structure may limit upside potential during periods of strong performance from smaller, riskier companies in the sector.
