Wall Street analysts are eyeing SoundHound AI (NASDAQ: SOUN) as a potential standout buy, particularly as market sentiment turns bearish on the artificial intelligence sector. Currently priced at $2.81, the stock has a one-year average price target of $14.62, suggesting a potential upside of 141%. This makes it an attractive option for investors looking to capitalize on undervalued opportunities in a fluctuating market.

SoundHound AI combines audio recognition with generative AI, targeting industries like restaurants, financial services, insurance, and healthcare. Its software is already automating processes in fast-food drive-thrus and is poised for further deployment across sectors that rely heavily on customer service. With a year-over-year revenue growth of 59%, the company is showing strong momentum, even as it remains unprofitable. At a price-to-sales ratio of 15, the stock appears reasonably valued given its growth potential.

For market professionals, SoundHound AI represents a compelling investment opportunity, especially if the company can successfully leverage its technology to capture market share in key industries.
