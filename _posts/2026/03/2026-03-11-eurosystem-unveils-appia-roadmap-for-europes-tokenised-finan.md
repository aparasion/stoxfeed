The Eurosystem has unveiled its Appia roadmap, a strategic initiative aimed at developing a tokenised financial ecosystem in Europe while maintaining the role of central bank money. This initiative will involve collaboration between the Eurosystem and various public and private sector stakeholders to create innovative and resilient wholesale financial markets, with a blueprint expected by 2028. 

The significance of Appia lies in its potential to enhance efficiency in wholesale financial markets through tokenisation and Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT). By bundling multiple asset lifecycle steps onto a single platform, the initiative could streamline processes from issuance to settlement, thereby fostering a more integrated and competitive financial environment in Europe.
