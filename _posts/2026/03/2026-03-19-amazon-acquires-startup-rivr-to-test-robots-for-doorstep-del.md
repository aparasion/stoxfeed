Amazon has officially acquired Rivr, a Swiss robotics firm specializing in doorstep delivery technology, as confirmed in a notice to its third-party delivery contractors. While financial terms were not disclosed, this acquisition underscores Amazon's ongoing commitment to enhancing its logistics capabilities, particularly in the critical last-mile delivery segment.

The move is significant for the financial markets as it aligns with Amazon's broader strategy to automate and improve efficiency in its delivery operations. With Rivr's innovative four-legged robots, Amazon aims to streamline the process of transporting packages from delivery vehicles to customers' doorsteps, potentially enhancing safety and customer satisfaction. This acquisition follows Amazon's substantial investments in robotics, including the deployment of over 1 million robots across its operations.
