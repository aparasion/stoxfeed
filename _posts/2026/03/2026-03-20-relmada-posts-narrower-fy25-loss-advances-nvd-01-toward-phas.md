Relmada Therapeutics, Inc. (RLMD) has reported significant advancements in its fourth-quarter and full-year 2025 results, highlighted by promising 12-month data for its bladder cancer candidate NDV-01 and a $160 million PIPE financing that extends its cash runway through 2029. The company emphasized the transformational nature of the past year, with NDV-01 showing a 95% complete response rate and a durable 76% rate at 12 months, positioning it favorably for late-stage development.

These developments are critical for financial markets as they underscore Relmada's potential for growth within the oncology sector, particularly with its FDA-cleared registrational pathways for NDV-01. The company’s reduced net loss and improved cash position signal a more stable financial outlook, which could enhance investor confidence and influence stock performance.
