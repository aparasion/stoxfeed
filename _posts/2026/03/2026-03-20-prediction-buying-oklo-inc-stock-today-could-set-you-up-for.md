Bank of America has identified nuclear energy as a burgeoning $10 trillion market, driven by increasing electricity demand, particularly from the AI sector. Their report highlights that nuclear power offers reliable baseload energy with a lower carbon footprint, positioning it as a crucial player in meeting the electricity needs of the future. As AI companies are projected to triple their electricity demand by 2030, the urgency for new energy sources, including nuclear, is becoming apparent.

Oklo Inc. (NYSE: OKLO) emerges as a compelling investment opportunity within this landscape. Following a significant price correction, Oklo’s market cap stands at $9.5 billion, a fraction of the potential market size. However, investors should be cautious of risks, including competition from established players and potential uranium supply constraints, which could impact operations.
