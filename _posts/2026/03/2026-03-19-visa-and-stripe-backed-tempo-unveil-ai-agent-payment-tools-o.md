Visa's crypto division has unveiled Visa CLI, a groundbreaking tool that enables AI agents to execute same-day payments, coinciding with the launch of Stripe-backed Tempo's blockchain and payments protocol. Cuy Sheffield, head of Visa Crypto Labs, highlighted that Visa CLI simplifies programmatic card payments while enhancing security by eliminating the need for sensitive API keys. This development comes amid growing interest in AI and stablecoin transactions, positioning Visa at the forefront of this evolving landscape.

The launch of these tools is significant for the financial markets as they signal a shift towards automated payments facilitated by AI, potentially reshaping transaction dynamics. With Coinbase's x402 standard and Tempo's Machine Payments Protocol gaining traction, the infrastructure for AI-driven financial interactions is rapidly expanding. This could lead to a substantial increase in the volume of transactions conducted by AI agents, outpacing traditional human payments.
