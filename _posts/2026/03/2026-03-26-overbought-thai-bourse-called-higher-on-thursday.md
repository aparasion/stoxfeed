The Stock Exchange of Thailand (SET) has posted notable gains, rising 4.2% over two sessions to close at 1,457.91. This upward momentum is supported by positive global market conditions, including easing oil prices and optimism surrounding a potential ceasefire in the Middle East. The SET's recent performance was bolstered by strong contributions from sectors such as food, finance, and technology, with 386 stocks advancing against 109 decliners.

The broader Asian markets are expected to follow suit, buoyed by a positive lead from Wall Street, where major indices also experienced gains amid a significant pullback in crude oil prices. Brent crude fell by 1.7%, alleviating some transport concerns, which could further support market sentiment across the region.

For market professionals, the key takeaway is the potential for continued bullish trends in the Thai market, particularly if global conditions remain favorable and sector-specific performance continues to strengthen. Monitoring developments in oil prices and geopolitical tensions will be crucial for assessing future market movements.
