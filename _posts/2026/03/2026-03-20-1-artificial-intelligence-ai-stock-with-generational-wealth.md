Nvidia (NVDA) continues to dominate the AI landscape, with its market capitalization soaring to $4.4 trillion, driven by its pivotal role in AI chip production. The company reported a staggering 65% revenue growth to nearly $216 billion for its fiscal year, with a forecasted 77% year-over-year increase for the current quarter. This growth is fueled by a projected expansion of the global AI chip market, expected to reach $1 trillion by 2030, where Nvidia holds a commanding 90% market share.

The implications for investors are significant, as Nvidia is not only capitalizing on AI chips but also venturing into the burgeoning physical AI sector, which contributed $6 billion to its revenue in fiscal 2026. With plans to invest $26 billion in open-weight AI models, Nvidia aims to establish itself as a full-stack AI company, further enhancing its growth trajectory.
