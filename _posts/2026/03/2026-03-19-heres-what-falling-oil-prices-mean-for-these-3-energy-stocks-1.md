EnerCom has announced the initial lineup of presenting companies for its 31st Annual Energy Investment Conference, scheduled for August 17–19, 2026, in Denver, Colorado. This event will bring together key stakeholders from the energy sector, including exploration and production (E&P), midstream, oilfield services (OFS), and energy transition firms, providing a platform for networking and investment opportunities.

The conference comes at a pivotal moment for the energy markets, as Brent crude prices have surged due to escalating geopolitical tensions in the Middle East. Analysts predict that if the conflict persists, oil prices could soar to $150 per barrel, reflecting significant supply disruptions and heightened market volatility. This environment presents both risks and opportunities for investors, particularly in sectors directly impacted by oil price fluctuations and energy infrastructure stability.

For professionals in the financial markets, this conference is a critical opportunity to gain insights into emerging trends and investment strategies within the energy sector.
