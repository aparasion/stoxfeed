Microelectronics US 2026 is set to convene industry leaders and engineers on April 22-23 in Austin, focusing on pressing challenges in the semiconductor and embedded systems sectors. The event's significance lies in its emphasis on actionable insights, featuring discussions led by engineers from major companies like Ford and Honeywell. Attendees will gain valuable perspectives on balancing performance and cost while navigating the complexities of modern microelectronics. Additionally, the exhibition will connect participants with solution providers across the ecosystem, showcasing innovations in AI, photonics, and manufacturing.
