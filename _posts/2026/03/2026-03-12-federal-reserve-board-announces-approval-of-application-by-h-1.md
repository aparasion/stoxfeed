The Federal Reserve Board has approved Home BancShares' application to acquire Mountain Commerce Bancorp, which includes Mountain Commerce Bank, based in Knoxville, Tennessee. This strategic move allows Home BancShares, headquartered in Conway, Arkansas, to enhance its market presence and operational footprint in the region.

This acquisition is significant as it could lead to increased competition in the banking sector, potentially impacting stock performance for both institutions involved.
