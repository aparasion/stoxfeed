The Vitalism Foundation has announced the return of Vitalist Bay 2026, the world's largest longevity festival, set to take place from May 14–17, 2026, at the Lighthaven Campus in Berkeley, California. This year’s event will focus on six key themes: Biotech, Investors, Replacement, Longevity Science, AI x Bio, and Biostasis, bringing together leading researchers, biotech founders, clinicians, and investors for an immersive experience aimed at advancing the longevity agenda.

Vitalist Bay 2026 promises an exceptional lineup of over 60 speakers and a rich program of hands-on workshops covering topics from epigenetic reprogramming to precision nutrition for healthspan. This festival not only fosters collaboration but also accelerates capital formation in the longevity sector through structured matchmaking between investors and startups, enhancing the potential for groundbreaking therapeutic advancements.

For professionals in aging biology and longevity therapeutics, Vitalist Bay 2026 represents a unique opportunity to engage with pioneering ideas and influential figures in the field. I encourage you to explore the full details of this exciting event at the original source.
