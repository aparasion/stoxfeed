Cymbiotika has partnered with Ulta Beauty to introduce its wellness supplements to a broader audience, marking a significant expansion into the beauty retail sector. The collaboration will kick off with an online launch on March 15, 2026, followed by in-store availability at over 1,000 Ulta locations starting March 29. This partnership highlights the increasing consumer interest in "beauty-from-within" products that enhance topical skincare routines.

The initial product lineup includes supplements targeting cellular health and recovery, such as NAD+ for healthy aging, Liposomal Glutathione for detoxification, and Liposomal Vitamin C for skin elasticity. This strategic move not only taps into the growing market for wellness-oriented beauty solutions but also positions Cymbiotika’s offerings within Ulta's expanding wellness category, emphasizing the connection between internal health and skin appearance.
