TransPerfect has reported a 7% revenue growth in 2025, reaching USD 1.32 billion, marking a significant shift as traditional language services now account for less than 50% of its total revenue for the first time. This change highlights the company's strategic pivot toward industry-specific solutions that integrate language services with technology and consulting, reflecting broader trends in the localization and language services sector.

The growth in non-translation business units, including media, legal, and AI consulting, underscores the increasing demand for comprehensive localization solutions. TransPerfect's investments in proprietary technology and AI have driven notable revenue increases, particularly in its GlobalLink platform, which saw an 18% rise in licensing revenue. The company is also focusing on responsible AI use, especially in legal contexts, as it navigates the complexities introduced by recent court rulings regarding document confidentiality.
