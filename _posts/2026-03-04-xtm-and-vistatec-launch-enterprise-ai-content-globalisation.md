XTM and Vistatec have launched a partnership to create an end-to-end enterprise AI globalisation service aimed at helping organizations streamline their global content production. This collaboration combines XTM's AI-driven globalisation platform with Vistatec's expertise in translation and localisation, enabling businesses to operationalize AI-driven content while ensuring governance, auditability, and quality control.

The new service addresses the challenges many enterprises face, such as inconsistent tools and processes across departments, which can lead to inefficiencies and compliance risks. By integrating these capabilities, the partnership allows companies to scale their global content efforts more effectively, reducing duplication of work and enhancing visibility into operational performance.

As AI technologies transition from pilot projects to regular use, this partnership positions globalisation as a key growth enabler for businesses, ensuring they can launch products faster while maintaining brand integrity.
