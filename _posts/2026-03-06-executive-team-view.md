RWS has announced a significant leadership update, appointing several key executives to strengthen its strategic direction and operational capabilities. Benjamin Faes has been appointed Group Chief Executive Officer, bringing over 25 years of experience in digital transformation and technology-driven businesses. His previous roles include senior positions at AOL, Google, and Webhelp, where he successfully drove growth and innovation.

Stephen Lamb has joined as Chief Financial Officer, responsible for global finance strategy and operations. He has extensive experience in corporate finance, having previously served as Group Finance Director at Midwich Group and CFO at Iron Mountain International. Joseph Ayala has been appointed Executive Vice President of Strategy and Corporate Affairs, focusing on strategy and M&A activities, while Jérôme Grateau will lead the Go-to-Market strategy.

These appointments are expected to enhance RWS's competitive position in the market, particularly in technology and content solutions, as the company aims for sustainable growth and innovation. For more details, please read the full article.
